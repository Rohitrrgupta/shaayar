sila in ki kad-o-kavish ka hai sinon ki be-nuri
yaqin paida kar ai nadan yaqin se haath aati hai

vo darveshi ki jis ke samne jhukti hai faghfuri
kabhi hairat kabhi masti kabhi ah-e-sahar-gahi

badalta hai hazaron rang mera dard-e-mahjuri
had-e-idrak se bahar hain baten ishq o masti ki

samajh men is qadar aaya ki dil ki maut hai duuri
vo apne husn ki masti se hain majbur-e-paidai

miri ankhon ki binai men hain asbab-e-masturi
koi taqdir ki mantiq samajh sakta nahin varna

na the turkan-e-usmani se kam turkan-e-taimuri
faqiran-e-haram ke haath 'iqbal' aa gaya kyunkar

mayassar miir o sultan ko nahin shahin-e-kafuri
