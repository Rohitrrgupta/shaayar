jurm-e-ummid ki saza hi de
mere haq men bhi kuchh suna hi de

ishq men ham nahin ziyada talab
tu ne taron se shab ki maang bhari

mujh ko ik ashk-e-subh-gahi de
tu ne banjar zamin ko phuul diye

mujh ko ik zakhm-e-dil-kusha hi de
bastiyon ko diye hain tu ne charagh

dasht-e-dil ko bhi koi raahi de
umr bhar ki nava-gari ka sila

ai khuda koi ham-nava hi de
zard-ru hain varaq khayalon ke

ai shab-e-hijr kuchh siyahi de
