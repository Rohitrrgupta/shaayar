zamane ki do-rangi ka use hargiz na gham hota
ye paband-e-musibat dil ke hathon ham to rahte hain

nahin to chain se kaTti na dil hota na gham hota
unhin ki be-vafai ka ye hai aThon-pahar sadma

vahi hote jo qaabu men to phir kaahe ko gham hota
lab-o-chashm-e-sanam gar dekhne paate kahin shair

koi shirin-sukhan hota koi jadu-raqam hota
bahut achchha hua aae na vo meri ayadat ko

jo vo aate to ghair aate jo ghair aate to gham hota
agar qabren nazar atin na dara-o-sikandar ki

liye jaata hai josh-e-shauq ham ko rah-e-ulfat men
nahin to zoaf se dushvar chalna do-qadam hota

na rahne paae divaron men rauzan shukr hai varna
tumhen to dil-lagi hoti gharibon par sitam hota
