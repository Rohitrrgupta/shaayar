tapish se meri vaqf-e-kashmakash har tar-e-bistar hai
mira sar ranj-e-balin hai mira tan bar-e-bistar hai

sarishk-e-sar ba-sahra daada nur-ul-ain-e-daman hai
khusha iqbal-e-ranjuri ayadat ko tum aae ho

abhi aati hai bu balish se us ki zulf-e-mushkin ki
hamari diid ko khvab-e-zulekha ar-e-bistar hai

kahun kya dil ki kya halat hai hijr-e-yar men ghalib
ki betabi se har-yak tar-e-bistar khar-e-bistar hai
