pariyon aisa ruup hai jis ka laḌkon aisa naanv
saare dhande chhoḌ-chhaḌ ke chaliye us ke gaanv

pakki saḌkon vaale shahr men kis se milne jaaen
haule se bhi paanv paḌe to baj uThti hain khaḌanv

aate hain khulta darvaza dekh ke ruk jaate hain
dil par naqsh biTha jaate hain yahi ThiTakte paanv

pyasa kavva jangal ke chashme men Duub mara
divana kar deti hai peḌon ki mahakti chhanv

abhi nai baazi hogi phir se patta Dalenge
koi baat nahin jo haar gae hain pahla daanv
