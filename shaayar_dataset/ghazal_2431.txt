garaj-baras pyasi dharti par phir paani de maula
chiḌiyon ko daane bachchon ko guḌ-dhani de maula

do aur do ka joḌ hamesha chaar kahan hota hai
soch samajh valon ko thoḌi nadani de maula

phir raushan kar zahr ka pyala chamka nai saliben
jhuTon ki duniya men sach ko tabani de maula

phir murat se bahar aa kar charon or bikhar ja
phir mandir ko koi 'mira' divani de maula

tere hote koi kisi ki jaan ka dushman kyuun ho
jiine valon ko marne ki asani de maula
