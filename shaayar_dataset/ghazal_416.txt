jaane kya dekha tha main ne khvab men
phans gaya phir jism ke girdab men

tera kya tu to baras ke khul gaya
mera sab kuchh bah gaya sailab men

meri ankhon ka bhi hissa hai bahut
tere is chehre ki ab-o-tab men

tujh men aur mujh men taalluq hai vahi
hai jo rishta saaz aur mizrab men

mera vaada hai ki saari zindagi
tujh se main milta rahunga khvab men
