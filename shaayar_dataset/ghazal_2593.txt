miTa sakegi use gard-e-mah-o-sal kahan
khinchi hui hai jo tasvir-e-yar ankhon men

mah o nujum ka saara ghubar ankhon men

basa hua hai vahi shah-savar ankhon men
vo ek tha pa kiya us ko jab tah-e-talvar

to baT gaya vahi chehra hazar ankhon men
