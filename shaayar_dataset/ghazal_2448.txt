shahr sunsan hai kidhar jaaen
khaak ho kar kahin bikhar jaaen

raat kitni guzar gai lekin
itni himmat nahin ki ghar jaaen

yuun tire dhyan se larazta huun
jaise patte hava se Dar jaaen

un ujalon ki dhun men phirta huun
chhab dikhate hi jo guzar jaaen

rain andheri hai aur kinara duur
chand nikle to paar utar jaaen
