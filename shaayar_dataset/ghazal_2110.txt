aa jaaen ham nazar jo koi dam bahut hai yaan
mohlat hamen bisan-e-sharar kam bahut hai yaan

yak lahza sina-kubi se fursat hamen nahin
yaani ki dil ke jaane ka matam bahut hai yaan

hasil hai kya sivae tarai ke dahr men
uTh asman tale se ki shabnam bahut hai yaan

mail-ba-ghair hona tujh abru ka aib hai
thi zor ye kaman vale kham-cham bahut hai yaan

ham rah-ravan-e-rah-e-fana der rah chuke
vaqfa bisan-e-subh koi dam bahut hai yaan

is but-kade men maani ka kis se karen saval
aadam nahin hai surat-e-adam bahut hai yaan

aalam men log milne ki gon ab nahin rahe
har-chand aisa vaisa to aalam bahut hai yaan

vaisa chaman se saada nikalta nahin koi
rangini ek aur kham-o-cham bahut hai yaan

ejaz-e-isvi se nahin bahas ishq men
teri hi baat jaan mujassam bahut hai yaan

mere halak karne ka gham hai abas tumhen
tum shaad zindagani karo gham bahut hai yaan

dil mat laga rukh-e-araq-alud yaar se
aine ko uTha ki zamin nam bahut hai yaan

shayad ki kaam subh tak apna khinche na 'mir'
ahval aaj shaam se darham bahut hai yaan
