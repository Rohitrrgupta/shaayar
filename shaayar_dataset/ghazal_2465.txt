kya kahen tum se bud-o-bash apni
kaam hi kya vahi talash apni

koi dam aisi zindagi bhi karen
apna siina ho aur kharash apni

zaat hai ab to paash paash apni
hai labon par nafas-zani ki dukan

yava-goi hai bas maash apni
teri surat pe huun nisar pa ab

aur surat koi tarash apni
jism o jaan ko to bech hi Daala

ab mujhe bechni hai laash apni
