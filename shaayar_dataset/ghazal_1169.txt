ye hai mai-kada yahan rind hain yahan sab ka saaqi imaam hai
ye haram nahin hai ai shaikh ji yahan parsai haram hai

jo zara si pi ke bahak gaya use mai-kade se nikal do
yahan tang-nazar ka guzar nahin yahan ahl-e-zarf ka kaam hai

koi mast hai koi tishna-lab to kisi ke haath men jaam hai
magar is pe koi kare bhi kya ye to mai-kade ka nizam hai

ye janab-e-shaikh ka falsafa hai ajiib saare jahan se
jo vahan piyo to halal hai jo yahan piyo to haram hai

isi kaenat men ai 'jigar' koi inqalab uThega phir
ki buland ho ke bhi aadmi abhi khvahishon ka ghulam hai
