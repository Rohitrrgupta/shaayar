main apne khvab se bichhḌa nazar nahin aata
to is sadi men akela nazar nahin aata

ajab dabao hai un bahari havaon ka
gharon ka bojh bhi uThta nazar nahin aata

main teri raah se haTne ko haT gaya lekin
mujhe to koi bhi rasta nazar nahin aata

main ik sada pe hamesha ko ghar to chhoḌ aaya
magar pukarne vaala nazar nahin aata

dhuan bhara hai yahan to sabhi ki ankhon men
kisi ko ghar mira jalta nazar nahin aata

ghazal-sarai ka daava to sab kare hain 'vasim'
magar vo 'mir' sa lahja nazar nahin aata
