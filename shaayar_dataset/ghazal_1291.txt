vo miri chin-e-jabin se gham-e-pinhan samjha
yak alif besh nahin saiqal-e-aina hanuz

chaak karta huun main jab se ki gareban samjha
is qadar tang hua dil ki main zindan samjha

bad-gumani ne na chaha use sargarm-e-khiram
rukh pe har qatra araq dida-e-hairan samjha

ijz se apne ye jaana ki vo bad-khu hoga
safar-e-ishq men ki zoaf ne rahat-talabi

har qadam saae ko main apne shabistan samjha
tha gurezan mizha-e-yar se dil ta dam-e-marg

daf-e-paikan-e-qaza is qadar asan samjha
dil diya jaan ke kyuun us ko vafadar 'asad'

ghalati ki ki jo kafir ko musalman samjha
ham ne vahshat-kada-e-bazm-e-jahan men juun shama

shoala-e-ishq ko apna sar-o-saman samjha
