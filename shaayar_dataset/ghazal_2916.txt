ek ek qatre ka mujhe dena paḌa hisab
ab main huun aur matam-e-yak-shahr-arazu

toḌa jo tu ne aaina timsal-dar tha
galiyon men meri na'sh ko khinche phiro ki main

mauj-e-sarab-e-dasht-e-vafa ka na puchh haal
har zarra misl-e-jauhar-e-tegh ab-dar tha

kam jante the ham bhi gham-e-ishq ko par ab
dekha to kam hue pa gham-e-rozgar tha

kis ka junun-e-did tamanna-shikar tha
kis ka khayal aina-e-intizar tha

har barg-e-gul ke parde men dil be-qarar tha
juun ghuncha-o-gul afat-e-fal-e-nazar na puchh

paikan se tere jalva-e-zakhm ashkar tha
subh-e-qayamat ek dum-e-gurg thi 'asad'

jis dasht men vo shokh-e-do-alam shikar tha
