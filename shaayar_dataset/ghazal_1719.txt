achchhi nahin ye khamushi shikva karo gila karo
yuun bhi na kar sako to phir ghar men khuda khuda karo

shohrat bhi us ke saath hai daulat bhi us ke haath hai
khud se bhi vo mile kabhi us ke liye dua karo

dekho ye shahr hai ajab dil bhi nahin hai kam ghazab
shaam ko ghar jo aauun main thoḌa sa saj liya karo

dil men jise basao tum chand use banao tum
vo jo kahe paḌha karo jo na kahe suna karo

meri nashist pe bhi kal aaega koi dusra
tum bhi bana ke rasta mere liye jagah karo
