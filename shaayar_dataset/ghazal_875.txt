zamin se ta-ba-falak dhund ki khudai hai
hava-e-shahr-e-junun kya payam laai hai

piya hai zahr bhi ab-e-hayat bhi lekin
kisi ne tishnagi-e-jism-o-jan bujhai hai

ye koi zid hai ki hai rasm-e-ashiqi hi yahi
hava ke rukh pe jo sham-e-vafa jalai hai

ye kam nahin ki taraf-dar hain mire kuchh log
hunar ki varna yahan kis ne daad paai hai

ki chand gaam hi ab manzil-e-judai hai
