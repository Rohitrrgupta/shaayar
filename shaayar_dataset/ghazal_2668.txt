dil jo zer-e-ghubar aksar tha
kuchh mizaj in dinon mukaddar tha

is pe takiya kiya to tha lekin
rat-din ham the aur bistar tha

sarsari tum jahan se guzre
varna har ja jahan-e-digar tha

dil ki kuchh qadr karte rahiyo tum
ye hamara bhi naz-parvar tha

baa'd yak 'umr jo hua ma'lum
dil us aina-ru ka patthar tha

baare sajda ada kiya tah-e-tegh
kab se ye bojh mere sar par tha

kyuun na abr-e-siyah safed hava
varna har ik qadam pe yaan ghar tha

be-zari ka na kar gila ghafil
rah tasalli ki yuun muqaddar tha

itne munim jahan men guzre
vaqt rehlat ke kis kane zar tha

ik azaan jumla ab sikandar tha
thi ye sab kaenat zer-e-nagin

saath mor-o-malakh sa lashkar tha
chahiye jis qadar mayassar tha

akhir-e-kar jab jahan se gaya
haath khali kafan se bahar tha

kya karun main sukhan se khugar tha
