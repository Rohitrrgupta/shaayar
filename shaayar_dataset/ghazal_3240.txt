dukh ki chikhen pyaar ki sargoshiyan rah jaengi
ham chale jaenge avazen yahan rah jaengi

ainon jaise badan dariya baha le jaega
ret men pairahanon ki dhajjiyan rah jaengi

subh se pahle guzar jaega ye tufan bhi
asman par ikka-dukka badliyan rah jaengi

ho bhi jaae dil agar khali khayal-o-khvab se
phir bhi ik kone men kuchh be-khvabiyan rah jaengi
