varna ashiq ko chashm-e-khiffat hai
ta dam-e-marg gham khushi ka nahin

dil men nasur phir jidhar chahe
rona aata hai dam-ba-dam shayad

kaso hasrat ki dil se rukhsat hai
fitne rahte hain us ke saae men

na tujhe rahm ne use Tuk sabr
dil pe mere ajab musibat hai

tu to nadan hai nipaT naseh
kab moassir tiri nasihat hai

dil pe jab mere aa ke ye Thahra
ki mujhe khush-dili aziyyat hai

ranj-o-mehnat se baaz kyunke rahun
vaqt jaata rahe to hasrat hai

kya hai phir koi dam ko kya jaano
dam ghanimat miyan jo fursat hai

tera shikva mujhe na mera tujhe
chahiye yuun jo fil-haqiqat hai

tujh ko masjid hai mujh ko mai-khana
vaiza apni apni qismat hai

aise hansmukh ko shama se tashbih
shama-e-majlis ki roni surat hai

teri ankhon ka sehr aafat hai
