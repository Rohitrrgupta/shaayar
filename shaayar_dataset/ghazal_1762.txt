na kisi se paas mere yaar ka aana gaya
I saw nothing besides the curving, leaping fire

bas gaya main jaan se ab us se ye jaana gaya
The last thing I saw was the moth moving to the fire

kuchh na dekha phir ba-juz yak shola-e-pur-pech-o-tab
shama tak to ham ne dekha tha ki parvana gaya

ek hi chashmak thi fursat sohbat-e-ahbab ki
dida-e-tar saath le majlis se paimana gaya

gul khile sad rang to kya be-pari se a nasim
muddaten guzrin ki vo gulzar ka jaana gaya

duur tujh se 'mir' ne aisa taab khincha ki shokh
kal jo main dekha use mutlaq na pahchana gaya
