DhunD raha hai farang aish-e-jahan ka davam
pir-e-haram ne kaha sun ke meri ruedad

pukhta hai teri fughan ab na ise dil men thaam
tha arini go kalim main arini go nahin

us ko taqaza rava mujh pe taqaza haram
garche hai ifsha-e-raz ahl-e-nazar ki fughan

ho nahin sakta kabhi sheva-e-rindana aam
halqa-e-sufi men zikr be-nam o be-soz-o-saz

main bhi raha tishna-kam tu bhi raha tishna-kam
ishq tiri intiha ishq miri intiha

tu bhi abhi na-tamam main bhi abhi na-tamam
aah ki khoya gaya tujh se faqiri ka raaz
