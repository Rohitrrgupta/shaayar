pae-nazr-e-karam tohfa hai sharm-e-na-rasai ka
na ho husn-e-tamasha-dost rusva bevafai ka

ba-mohr-e-sad-nazar sabit hai daava parsai ka
zakat-e-husn de ai jalva-e-binish ki mehr-asa

charagh-e-khana-e-darvesh ho kaasa gadai ka
na maara jaan kar be-jurm ghafil teri gardan par

raha manind-e-khun-e-be-gunah haq ashnai ka
miTa jis se taqaza shikva-e-be-dast-o-pai ka

vahi ik baat hai jo yaan nafas vaan nikhat-e-gul hai
chaman ka jalva baais hai miri rangin-navai ka

adam tak bevafa charcha hai teri bevafai ka
na de naale ko itna tuul 'ghalib' mukhtasar likh de

ki hasrat-sanj huun arz-e-sitam-ha-e-judai ka
jahan miT jaae sai-e-did khizrabad-e-asaish

ba-jeb-e-har-nigah pinhan hai hasil rahnumai ka
taghaful ko na kar masruf-e-tamkin-azmai ka

'asad' ka qissa tulani hai lekin mukhtasar ye hai
ki hasrat-kash raha arz-e-sitam-ha-e-judai ka

ba-jeb-e-arzu pinhan hai hasil dilrubai ka
raha begana-e-tasir afsun ashnai ka

savad-e-khatt-e-peshani se nuskha momyai ka
jise tu bandagi kahta hai daava hai khudai ka
