kya din the ve ki yaan bhi dil armida tha
qasid jo vaan se aaya to sharminda main hua

bechara girya-nak gareban darida tha
ik vaqt ham ko tha sar-e-girya ki dasht men

jo khaar khushk tha sau vo tufan rasida tha
jis said-gah-e-ishq men yaron ka ji gaya

marg us shikar-gah ka shikar ramida tha
kori chashm kyuun na ziyarat ko us ki aae

yusuf sa jis ko madd-e-nazar nur-dida tha
afsos marg sabr hai is vaste ki vo

mat puchh kis tarah se kaTi raat hijr ki
har naala meri jaan ko tegh kashida tha

hasil na puchh gulshan-e-mashhad ka bul-havas
yaan phal har ik darakht ka halq-e-burida tha

dil-e-be-qarar girya-e-khunin tha raat 'mir'
aaya nazar to bismil dar-e-khun tapida tha
