khil gai manind-e-gul sau ja se divar-e-chaman
ulfat-e-gul se ghalat hai dava-e-varastagi

saaf hai az bas-ki aks-e-gul se gulzar-e-chaman
hai nazakat bas ki fasl-e-gul men meamar-e-chaman

qalib-e-gul men Dhali hai khisht-e-divar-e-chaman
teri araish ka istiqbal karti hai bahar

bas ki paai yaar ki rangin-adai se shikast
vaqt hai gar bulbul-e-miskin zulekhai kare
