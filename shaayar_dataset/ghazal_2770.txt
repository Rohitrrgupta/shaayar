miTe vo dil jo tire gham ko le ke chal na sake
vahi charagh bujhae gae jo jal na sake

ham is liye nai duniya ke saath chal na sake
ki jaise rang ye badli hai ham badal na sake

main un charaghon ki umr-e-vafa ko rota huun
jo ek shab bhi mire dil ke saath jal na sake

main vo musafir-e-ghamgin huun jis ke saath 'vasim'
khizan ke daur bhi kuchh duur chal ke chal na sake
