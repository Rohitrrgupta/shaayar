chashma-e-bad-mast ko phir sheva-e-dil-dari de
ishq hai sada-o-masum use apni tarah

jo dukhe dil hain unhen daulat-e-darman ho ata
dard ke haath men mat kasa-e-nadari de

kitni farsuda hai ye jurm-o-saza ki duniya
sar-kashi dil ko naya zauq-e-gunahgari de

shakh-e-gul kab se hai siine men chhupae hue gul
dekhen kab bad-e-saba hukm-e-chaman-kari de

raat akhir hai use jashn-e-sharar-bari de
chaman afsurda hai ai jan-e-chaman ruh-e-bahar

gul ko bhi apne tabassum ki fusun-kari de
