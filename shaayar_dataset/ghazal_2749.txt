jab ba-taqrib-e-safar yaar ne mahmil bandha
tapish-e-shauq ne har zarre pe ik dil bandha

yaas o ummid ne yak-arbada maidan manga
na bandhe tishnagi-e-zauq ke mazmun 'ghalib'

garche dil khol ke dariya ko bhi sahil bandha
jo girah aap na kholi use mushkil bandha

yaar ne tishnagi-e-shauq ke mazmun chahe
ham ne dil khol ke dariya ko bhi sahil bandha

diida ta-dil hai yak-aina charaghan kis ne
mutrib-e-dil ne mire tar-e-nafas se 'ghalib'

saaz par rishta pae naghma-e-'bedil' bandha
rang ne aaina ankhon ke muqabil bandha

nok-e-har-khar se tha bas-ki sar-e-duzdi-e-zakhm
chuun namad ham ne kaf-e-pa pe 'asad' dil bandha
