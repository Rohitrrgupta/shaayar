mila to manzil-e-jan men utarne na diya
vo kho gaya to kisi ne pukarne na diya

ravan davan hai yunhi kashti-e-zaman ab bhi
magar vo lamha jo dil ne guzarne na diya

lagi thi jaan ki baazi bisat ulaT Daali
ye khel bhi hamen yaron ne harne na diya

koi sada mire sabr-o-sukut se na uThi
koi maza tire qaul-o-qarar ne na diya

jo ek phuul kisi nau-bahar ne na diya
