main kisi shakhs se bezar nahin ho sakta
ek zarra bhi to be-kar nahin ho sakta

is qadar pyaar hai insan ki khataon se mujhe
ki farishta mira meaar nahin ho sakta

ai khuda phir ye jahannam ka tamasha kiya hai
tera shahkar to finnar nahin ho sakta

ai haqiqat ko faqat khvab samajhne vaale
tu kabhi sahab-e-asrar nahin ho sakta

tu ki ik mauja-e-nikhat se bhi chaunk uThta hai
hashr aata hai to bedar nahin ho sakta

sar-e-divar ye kyuun nirkh ki takrar hui
ghar ka angan kabhi bazar nahin ho sakta

raakh si majlis-e-aqvam ki chuTki men hai kya
kuchh bhi ho ye mira pindar nahin ho sakta

is haqiqat ko samajhne men luTaya kya kuchh
mera dushman mira gham-khvar nahin ho sakta

main ne bheja tujhe aivan-e-hukumat men magar
ab to barson tira didar nahin ho sakta

tirgi chahe sitaron ki sifarish laae
raat se mujh ko sarokar nahin ho sakta

vo jo sheron men hai ik shai pas-e-alfaz 'nadim'
us ka alfaz men izhar nahin ho sakta
