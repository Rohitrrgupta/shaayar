toḌ kar ahd-e-karam na-ashna ho jaiye
banda-parvar jaiye achchha khafa ho jaiye

mere uzr-e-jurm par mutlaq na kiije iltifat
balki pahle se bhi baḌh kar kaj-ada ho jaiye

khatir-e-mahrum ko kar dijiye mahv-e-alam
raah men miliye kabhi mujh se to az-rah-e-sitam

honT apna kaaT kar fauran juda ho jaiye
gar nigah-e-shauq ko mahv-e-tamasha dekhiye

qahr ki nazron se masruf-e-saza ho jaiye
meri tahrir-e-nadamat ka na diije kuchh javab

dekh liije aur taghaful-ashna ho jaiye
mujh se tanhai men gar miliye to diije galiyan

aur bazm-e-ghair men jan-e-haya ho jaiye
haan yahi meri vafa-e-be-asar ki hai saza

aap kuchh is se bhi baḌh kar pur-jafa ho jaiye
ji men aata hai ki us shokh-e-taghaful-kesh se

ab na miliye phir kabhi aur bevafa ho jaiye
kavish-e-dard-e-jigar ki lazzaton ko bhuul kar

mail-e-aram o mushtaq-e-shifa ho jaiye
ek bhi arman na rah jaae dil-e-mayus men

yaani akhir be-niyaz-e-muddaa ho jaiye
bhuul kar bhi us sitam-parvar ki phir aae na yaad

is qadar begana-e-ahd-e-vafa ho jaiye
haae ri be-ikhtiyari ye to sab kuchh ho magar

us sarapa naaz se kyunkar khafa ho jaiye
chahta hai mujh ko tu bhule na bhulun main tujhe

tere is tarz-e-taghaful ke fida ho jaiye
kashmakash-ha-e-alam se ab ye 'hasrat' ji men hai

chhuT ke in jhagḌon se mehman-e-qaza ho jaiye
