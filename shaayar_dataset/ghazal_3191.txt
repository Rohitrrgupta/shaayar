yuun bhi hota hai ki yak dam koi achchha lag jaae
baat kuchh bhi na ho aur dil men tamasha lag jaae

ham savalat ka hal soch rahe hon abhi tak
aur mathe pe mohabbat ka natija lag jaae

abhi divar uThai bhi na ho dil ki taraf
lekin is men koi dar koi daricha lag jaae

kya sitam hai ki vahi duur raha ho tum se
aur usi shakhs pe ilzam tumhara lag jaae

puuri avaz se ik roz pukarun tujh ko
aur phir meri zaban par tira taala lag jaae

aur to is ke sava kuchh nahin imkan ki ab
mere dariya men kahin tera kinara lag jaae

main ne aur dil ne isi baab men socha hai ki ham
kaam kuchh bhi na karen koi vazifa lag jaae

kya tamasha hai ki baaqi ho samundar ka safar
aur sahil se kisi roz safina lag jaae

ye bhi mumkin hai ki is kar-gah-e-dil men 'zafar'
kaam koi kare aur naam kisi ka lag jaae
