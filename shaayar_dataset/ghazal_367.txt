kal jinhen zindagi thi raas bahut
aaj dekha unhen udaas bahut

raftagan ka nishan nahin milta
ik rahi hai zamin ghaas bahut

kyuun na rouun tiri judai men
din guzare hain tere paas bahut

chhanv mil jaae daman-e-gul ki
hai gharibi men ye libas bahut

vadi-e-dil men paanv dekh ke rakh
hai yahan dard ki ugaas bahut

sukhe patton ko dekh kar 'nasir'
yaad aati hai gul ki baas bahut
