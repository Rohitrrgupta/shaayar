ye kya hua ki tabiat sambhalti jaati hai
tire baghair bhi ye raat Dhalti jaati hai

us ik ufuq pe abhi tak hai e'tibar mujhe
magar nigah-e-manazir badalti jaati hai

chahar samt se ghera hai tez andhi ne
kisi charagh ki lau phir bhi jalti jaati hai

main apne jism ki sargoshiyon ko sunta huun
tire visal ki saaat nikalti jaati hai

ye dekho aa gai mere zaval ki manzil
main ruk gaya miri parchhain chalti jaati hai
