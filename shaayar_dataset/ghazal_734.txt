ki TuuT jaate hain khud dil ke saath paimane
mili jab un se nazar bas raha tha ek jahan

haTi nigah to charon taraf the virane
hayat laghzish-e-paiham ka naam hai saaqi

labon se jaam laga bhi sakun khuda jaane
tabassumon ne nikhara hai kuchh to saaqi ke

kuchh ahl-e-gham ke sanvare hue hain mai-khane
ye aag aur nahin dil ki aag hai nadan

charagh ho ki na ho jal-bujhenge parvane
sharab ek hai badle hue hain paimane
