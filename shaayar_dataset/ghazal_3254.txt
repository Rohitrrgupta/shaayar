jahan ab khar-zaren ho gai hain
yahin aage baharen ho gai hain

junun men khushk ho rag-ha-e-gardan
gareban ki si taren ho gai hain

suna jaata hai shahr-e-ishq ke gird
mazaren hi mazaren ho gai hain

isi dariya-e-khubi ka hai ye shauq
ki maujen sab kanaren ho gai hain

inhin galiyon men jab rote the ham 'mir'
kai dariya ki dharen ho gai hain
