hijr-shab men ik qarar-e-ghaebana chahiye
ghaib men ik surat-e-mah-e-shabana chahiye

sun rahe hain jis ke charche shahr ki khilqat se ham
ja ke ik din us hasin ko dekh aana chahiye

is tarah aghaz shayad ik hayat-e-nau ka ho
pichhli saari zindagi ko bhuul jaana chahiye

vo jahan hi dusra hai vo bahut der-ashna
is jahan men us se milne ko zamana chahiye
