darbar men ab satvat-e-shahi ki alamat
darban ka asa hai ki musannif ka qalam hai

avara hai phir koh-e-nida par jo basharat
tamhid-e-masarrat hai ki tul-e-shab-e-gham hai

jis dhajji ko galiyon men liye phirte hain tiflan
ye mera gareban hai ki lashkar ka alam hai

jis nuur se hai shahr ki divar darakhshan
ye khun-e-shahidan hai ki zar-khana-e-jam hai

halqa kiye baiThe raho ik shama ko yaaro
kuchh raushni baaqi to hai har-chand ki kam hai
