zaban hai na-tavani se agar band
mire dil par nahin ma'ni ke dar band

hamari bekasi kab tak chhupegi
khuda par to nahin rah-e-khabar band

kiya ham ne bhi ab milne ka dar band
dilon men dard hi ki kuchh kami hai

nahin hai aah par rah-e-asar band
kamar hi jab nahin kaisa kamar-band

kahunga marsiya is gham men aisa
khule ma'ni dikhae jis ka har band

mira dil ab hai siine men nazar-band
