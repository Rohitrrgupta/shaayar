laghar hain jism rang hain kaale paḌe hue
balon pe gard paanv men chhale paḌe hue

is maarke men ishq bechara karega kya
khud husn ko hain jaan ke laale paḌe hue

ye jo bhi ho bahar nahin hai janab-e-man
kis vahm men hain dekhne vaale paḌe hue

is dahr ki kushada-dari par na jaiye
andar qadam qadam pe hain taale paḌe hue

shishe ke ik gilas men nargis ke phuul hain
ik mez par hain chand risale paḌe hue
