akhirash khuun thukne se miyan

baat men teri kya asar aaya
tha khabar men ziyan dil o jaan ka

har taraf se main be-khabar aaya
ab yahan hosh men kabhi apne

nahin aunga main agar aaya
main raha umr bhar juda khud se

yaad main khud ko umr bhar aaya
vo jo dil naam ka tha ek nafar

aaj main is se bhi mukar aaya
muddaton baad ghar gaya tha main

jaate hi main vahan se Dar aaya
