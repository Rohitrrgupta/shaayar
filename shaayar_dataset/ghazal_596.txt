yuun bahar aai hai is baar ki jaise qasid
kucha-e-yar se be-nail-e-maram aata hai

har koi shahr men phirta hai salamat-daman
rind mai-khane se shaista-khiram aata hai

abr aata hai kabhi mah-e-tamam aata hai
shauq valon ki hazin mahfil-e-shab men ab bhi

amad-e-subh ki surat tira naam aata hai
ab bhi elan-e-sahar karta hua mast koi

dagh-e-dil kar ke farozan sar-e-sham aata hai
