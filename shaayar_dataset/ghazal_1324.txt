Dara ke mauj o talatum se ham-nashinon ko
yahi to hain jo Duboya kiye safinon ko

sharab ho hi gai hai ba-qadr-e-paimana
ba-azm-e-tark nichoḌa jab astinon ko

miri nigah bhi deta khuda hasinon ko
hamari raah men aae hazar mai-khane

bhula sake na magar hosh ke qarinon ko
kabhi nazar bhi uThai na su-e-bada-e-nab

kabhi chaḌha gae pighla ke abginon ko
yahi jahan hai jahannam yahi jahan firdaus

batao alam-e-bala ke sair-binon ko
hue hain qafile zulmat ki vadiyon men ravan

charagh-e-rah kiye khun-chakan jabinon ko
tujhe na maane koi tujh ko is se kya 'majruh'

chal apni raah bhaTakne de nukta-chinon ko
