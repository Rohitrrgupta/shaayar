hamen amad-e-'mir' kal bha gai
tarah is men majnun ki sab pa gai

kahan ka ghubar-e-ah dil men ye tha
miri khaak badli si sab chha gai

kya paas bulbul-e-khizan ne na kuchh
hui samne yuun to ek ek ke

hamen se vo kuchh aankh sharma gai
jigar munh tak aate nahin bolte

gharaz ham bhi karte hain kya kya gai
na ham-rah koi na kasi se gaya

miri laash ta-gor tanha gai
ghaTa shama saan kyuun na jaun chala

tab-e-gham jigar ko mire kha gai
koi rahne vaali hai jaan aziiz

gai gar na imroz farda gai
kiye dast-o-pa gum jo 'mir' aa gaya

vafa-pesha majlis use pa gai
