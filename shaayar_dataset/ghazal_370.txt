kisi bhi shahr men jaao kahin qayam karo
koi faza koi manzar kisi ke naam karo

dua salam zaruri hai shahr valon se
magar akele men apna bhi ehtiram karo

hamesha amn nahin hota fakhtaon men
kabhi-kabhar uqabon se bhi kalam karo

har ek basti badalti hai rang-rup kai
jahan bhi subh guzaro udhar hi shaam karo

khuda ke hukm se shaitan bhi hai aadam bhi
vo apna kaam karega tum apna kaam karo
