hal-e-dil 'mir' ka ro ro ke sab ai maah suna
shab ko al-qissa ajab qissa-e-jan-kah suna

na-balad ho ke rah-e-ishq men pahunchun to kahin
hamra khizar ko yaan kahte hain gumrah suna

koi in tauron se guzre hai tire gham men miri
gaah tu ne na suna haal mira gaah suna

khvab-e-ghaflat men hain yaan sab to abas jaaga 'mir'
be-khabar dekha unhen main jinhen agah suna
