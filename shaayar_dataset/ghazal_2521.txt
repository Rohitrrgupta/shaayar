aaj zara fursat paai thi aaj use phir yaad kiya
band gali ke akhiri ghar ko khol ke phir abad kiya

khol ke khiḌki chand hansa phir chand ne donon hathon se
rang uḌaae phuul khilae chiḌiyon ko azad kiya

baḌe baḌe gham khaḌe hue the rasta roke rahon men
chhoTi chhoTi khushiyon se hi ham ne dil ko shaad kiya

baat bahut maamuli si thi ulajh gai takraron men
ek zara si zid ne akhir donon ko barbad kiya

danaon ki baat na maani kaam aai nadani hi
suna hava ko paḌha nadi ko mausam ko ustad kiya
