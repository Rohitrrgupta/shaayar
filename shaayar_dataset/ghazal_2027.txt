yaaro kisi qatil se kabhi pyaar na mango
apne hi gale ke liye talvar na mango

gir jaoge tum apne masiha ki nazar se
mar kar bhi ilaj-e-dil-e-bimar na mango

khul jaega is tarah nigahon ka bharam bhi
kanTon se kabhi phuul ki mahkar na mango

sach baat pe milta hai sada zahr ka piyala
jiina hai to phir jiine ka izhar na mango

us chiiz ka kya zikr jo mumkin hi nahin hai
sahra men kabhi saya-e-divar na mango
