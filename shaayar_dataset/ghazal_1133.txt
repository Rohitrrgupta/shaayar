khvab-o-khayal-e-gul se kidhar jaae aadmi
ik gulshan-e-hava hai jidhar jaae aadmi

dekhe hue se lagte hain raste makan makin
jis shahr men bhaTak ke jidhar jaae aadmi

dekhe hain vo nagar ki abhi tak huun khauf men
vo suraten mili hain ki Dar jaae aadmi

gahraiyon men is ki agar jaae aadmi
parde men rang-o-bu ke safar-dar-safar 'munir'

in manzilon se kaise guzar jaae aadmi
