latafat be-kasafat jalva paida kar nahin sakti
chaman zangar hai aina-e-bad-e-bahari ka

jahan saaqi ho tu batil hai daava hoshiyari ka
bahar-e-rang-e-khun-e-gul hai saman ashk-bari ka

junun-e-barq nashtar hai rag-e-abr-e-bahari ka
bandha hai uqda-e-khatir se paiman khaksari ka

nigah ko ablon se shaghl hai akhtar-shumari ka
'asad' saghar-kash-e-taslim ho gardish se gardun ki

ki nang-e-fahm-e-mastan hai gila bad-rozg-ari ka
