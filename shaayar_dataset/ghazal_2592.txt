ab to de Daala use allah jo chahe kare

shaikh ki mantiq ho ya chashm-e-fusun-saz-e-butan
sidha-sada huun mujhe gumrah jo chahe kare

dekh kar pothi barahman kahte hain is 'ahd men
shadi to asan nahin haan byaah jo chahe kare

kharch ki tafsil puchhunga na mangunga hisab
le le vo but kul miri tankhvah jo chahe kare

achchhe achchhe phans gae hain naukri ke jaal men
sach ye hai afzuni-e-tankhvah jo chahe kare

ba-asar hona to hai mauquf dil ke rang par
josh men yuun aa ke 'akbar' aah jo chahe kare
