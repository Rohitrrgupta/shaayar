jalva-saman hai rang-o-bu ham se
is chaman ki hai aabru ham se

dars lete hain khush-khirami ka
phuul milte hain ba-vazu ham se

ham se raushan hai kar-gah-e-sukhan
nafas-e-gul hai mushkbu ham se

shab ki tanhaiyon men pichhle pahar
chand karta hai guftugu ham se

shahr men ab hamare charche hain
jagmagate hain kakh-o-ku ham se
