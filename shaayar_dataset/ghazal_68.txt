vufur-e-shauq ki rangin hikayaten mat puchh
labon ka pyaar nigah ki shikayaten mat puchh

kisi nigah ki nas nas men tairte nishtar
vo ibtida-e-mohabbat ki rahaten mat puchh

vo nim-shab vo javan husn vo vufur-e-niyaz
nigah o dil ne jo ki hain ibadaten mat puchh

hujum-e-gham men bhi jiina sikha diya ham ko
gham-e-jahan ki hain kya kya inayaten mat puchh

ye sirf ek qayamat hai chain ki karvaT
dabi hain dil men hazaron qayamaten mat puchh

bas ek harf-e-baghavat zaban se nikla tha
shahid ho gaiin kitni rivayaten mat puchh

ab aaj qissa-e-dara-o-jam ka kya hoga
hamare paas hain apni hikayaten mat puchh

jo ibraton ne likhi hain ibaraten mat puchh
nashat-e-zist faqat ahl-e-gham ki hai miras

milengi aur abhi kitni daulaten mat puchh
