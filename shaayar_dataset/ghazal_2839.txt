mujhe andhere men be-shak biTha diya hota
magar charagh ki surat jala diya hota

na raushni koi aati mire taaqub men
jo apne-ap ko main ne bujha diya hota

ye dard jism ke ya-rab bahut shadid lage
mujhe salib pe do pal sula diya hota

ye shukr hai ki mire paas tera gham to raha
vagarna zindagi ne to rula diya hota
