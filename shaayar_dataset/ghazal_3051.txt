bahut sahi gham-e-giti sharab kam kya hai
ghulam-e-saqi-e-kausar huun mujh ko gham kya hai

tumhari tarz-o-ravish jante hain ham kya hai
raqib par hai agar lutf to sitam kya hai

sukhan men khama-e-ghalib ki atish-afshani
yaqin hai ham ko bhi lekin ab us men dam kya hai

kaTe to shab kahen kaaTe to saanp kahlave
koi batao ki vo zulf-e-kham-ba-kham kya hai

kise khabar hai ki vaan jumbish-e-qalam kya hai
na hashr-o-nashr ka qaail na kesh o millat ka

khuda ke vaste aise ki phir qasam kya hai
vo dad-o-did garan-maya shart hai hamdam
