nafas na anjuman-e-arzu se bahar khinch
agar sharab nahin intizar-e-saghar khinch

ba-rang-e-khar mire aaine se jauhar khinch
tujhe bahana-e-rahat hai intizar ai dil

kiya hai kis ne ishara ki naz-e-bistar khinch
tiri taraf hai ba-hasrat nazara-e-nargis

mire qadah men hai sahba-e-atish-e-pinhan
na kah ki taqat-e-rusvai-e-visal nahin

agar yahi araq-e-fitna hai mukarrar khinch
hamare safhe pe bal-e-pari se mistar khinch

khumar-e-minnat-e-saqi agar yahi hai 'asad'
Dil-e-gudakhta ke mai-kade men saghar khinch
