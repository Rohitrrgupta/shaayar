phir vahi lambi do-pahren-surmai hain phir vahi dil ki halat hai
bahar kitna sannaTa hai andar kitni vahshat hai

shaam karen kaise is din ki ThanDi surat dekhen kin ki
idhar udhar to dhuan uḌati aag ugalti khilqat hai

jis ko ham ne chaha tha vo kahin nahin is manzar men
jis ne ham ko pyaar kiya vo samne vaali murat hai

phuul babul ke achchhe hain lekin sakit tasviron men
sach much ke sehraon ki to is dil jaisi surat hai

tere baad dukanon par main ja kar puchhta rahta huun
kya vo khushbu mil sakti hai ab us ki kya qimat hai

baḌe baḌe sapne nahin boe main ne apne angan men
nannhi munni khushiyan hain miri chhoTi si ik jannat hai
