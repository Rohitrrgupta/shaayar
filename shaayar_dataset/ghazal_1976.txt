rabt har bazm se TuuTe tiri mahfil ke siva
ranjishen sab ki gavara hain tire dil ke siva

aise pahlu men sama jaao ki jaise dil ho
chain milta hai kahan mauj ko sahil ke siva

chikh Takra ke pahaḌon se palaT aati hai
kaun sahta hai bhala vaar muqabil ke siva

khushk patton se chhuḌa leti hain shakhen daman
kis ne yadon se nibhai hai yahan dil ke siva

ek bichhḌe hue saae ke taaqub men 'bashar'
sabhi rahon pe gae ham rah-e-manzil ke siva
