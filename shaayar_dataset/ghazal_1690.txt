bar-ha dil ko main samjha ke kaha kya kya kuchh
na suna aur khoya mujh se mira kya kya kuchh

izzat-o-abru o hurmat o diin o iman
rouun kis kis ko main yaaro ki gaya kya kya kuchh

sabr o aram kahun ya ki main ab hosh-o-havas
ho gaya us ki judai men juda kya kya kuchh

ishq kis zaat ka aqrab hai ki lagte hi niish
dil ke saath ankhon se paani ho baha kya kya kuchh

sada-rui ne to khoya dil o diin se dekhen
khat ke aane men hai qismat ka likha kya kya kuchh

dakhl kya rah-e-mohabbat men niko-nami ko
aaya is kuche men jo un ne suna kya kya kuchh

valih-o-shefta o zar-o-hazin o majnun
apne ashiq ko kal us ne na kaha kya kya kuchh

girya-e-shisha kabhi tha to kabhi khanda-e-jam
saaqi is daur men tere na hua kya kya kuchh

ghurra mat ho jo zamane se tiri ban aai
tha vo kya kya ki na bigḌa na bana kya kya kuchh

shadi aane ki na kar yaar na jaane ka gham
aaya kya kya na kuchh is ja na gaya kya kya kuchh

siina qanun o ghina naala o dil hai mizrab
nikle hai saz-e-mohabbat se sada kya kya kuchh

dosto haq men taraqqi o tanazzul apne
kya kahen ham ko zamane se hua kya kya kuchh

zoaf o na-taqati o susti o aaza-shikani
ek ghanTe men javani ke baḌha kya kya kuchh

sair ki qudarat-e-khaliq ki butan men 'sauda'
musht bhar khaak men jalva hai bhala kya kya kuchh
