nahin rok sakoge jism ki in parvazon ko
baḌi bhuul hui jo chheḌ diya kai sazon ko

koi naya makin nahin aaya to hairat kya
kabhi tum ne khula chhoḌa hi nahin darvazon ko

kabhi paar bhi kar paengi sukut ke sahra ko
darpesh hai kitna aur safar avazon ko

mujhe kuchh logon ki rusvai manzur nahin
nahin aam kiya jo main ne apne razon ko

kahin ho na gai ho zamin parindon se khali
khule asman par dekhta huun phir bazon ko
