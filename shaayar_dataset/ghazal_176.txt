ye dil bhi daman-e-yusuf hai chaak chahta hai
duaen do mire qatil ko tum ki shahr ka shahr

usi ke haath se hona halak chahta hai
fasana-go bhi kare kya ki har koi sar-e-bazm

idhar udhar se kai aa rahi hain avazen
aur us ka dhyan bahut inhimak chahta hai

zara si gard-e-havas dil pe lazmi hai 'faraz'
vo ishq kya hai jo daman ko paak chahta hai
