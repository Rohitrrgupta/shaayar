haath se napta huun dard ki gahrai ko
ye naya khel mila hai miri tanhai ko

tha jo siine men charagh-e-dil-e-pur-khun na raha
chaTiye baiTh ke ab sabr-o-shakebai ko

dil-e-afsurda kisi tarah bahalta hi nahin
kya karen aap ki is hausla-afzai ko

khair badnam to pahle bhi bahut the lekin
tujh se milna tha ki par lag gae rusvai ko

nigah-e-naz na milte hue ghabra ham se
ham mohabbat nahin kahne ke shanasai ko

dil hai nairangi-e-ayyam pe hairan ab tak
itni si baat bhi ma'lum nahin bhaai ko
