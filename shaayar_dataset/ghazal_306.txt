hai adam men ghuncha mahv-e-ibrat-e-anjam-e-gul
yak-jahan zaanu taammul dar-qafa-e-khanda hai

varna dandan dar dil afshurdan bina-e-khanda hai
shorish-e-batin ke hain ahbab munkir varna yaan

dil muhit-e-girya o lab ashna-e-khanda hai
har shikast-e-qimat-e-dil men sada-e-khanda hai

naqsh-e-ibrat dar nazar ya naqd-e-ishrat dar bisat
subh o shabnam fursat-e-nashv-o-numa-e-khanda hai
