sahn ke naam pe sahra chahun
kaenaten mire khvabon ki asiir

aur qudrat se main kitna chahun
tarbiyat miri zamin ne ki hai

men khalaon men lapakna chahun
jitne tarik gharaunde hain vahan

dil ki qindil jalana chahun
phir se firdaus men jaana chahun

dozakh insan pe ho jaae haram
rab se ye vada-e-farda chahun

khushk patte na shajar se chhine
bas ye ehsan hava ka chahun

miri zid kaun karega puuri
shaam ko subh ka taara chahun

mira har kaam alag duniya se
jis ko chahun use tanha chahun

hijr ki kitni tamazat hai 'nadim'
ab kisi yaad ka saaya chahun
