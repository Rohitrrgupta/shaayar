hijr ki ankhon se ankhen to milate jaiye
hijr men karna hai kya ye to batate jaiye

ban ke khushbu ki udasi rahiye dil ke baagh men
duur hote jaiye nazdik aate jaiye

jaate jaate aap itna kaam to kiije mira
yaad ka saara sar-o-saman jalate jaiye

rah gai ummid to barbad ho jaunga main
jaiye to phir mujhe sach-much bhulate jaiye

zindagi ki anjuman ka bas yahi dastur hai
baḌh ke miliye aur mil kar duur jaate jaiye

akhirash rishta to ham men ik khushi ik gham ka tha
muskurate jaiye aansu bahate jaiye

vo gali hai ik sharabi chashm-e-kafir ki gali
us gali men jaiye to laḌkhaḌate jaiye

aap ko jab mujh se shikva hi nahin koi to phir
aag hi dil men lagani hai lagate jaiye

kuuch hai khvabon se taabiron ki samton men to phir
jaiye par dam-ba-dam barbad jaate jaiye

aap ka mehman huun main aap mere mezban
so mujhe zahr-e-muravvat to pilate jaiye

hai sar-e-shab aur mire ghar men nahin koi charagh
aag to is ghar men janana lagate jaiye
