dard-e-dil ki unhen khabar na hui
koi tadbir kargar na hui

koshishen ham ne kiin hazar magar
ishq men ek mo'tabar na hui

kar chuke ham ko be-gunah shahid
aap ki aankh phir bhi tar na hui

duur un se jo be-asar na hui
aai bujhne ko apni sham-e-hayat

shab-e-gham ki magar sahar na hui
subh ik ah-e-sard sar na hui

tum se kyunkar vo chhup sake 'hasrat'
nigah-e-shauq parda dar na hui
