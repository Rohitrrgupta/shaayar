aaj in khanqahon men hai faqat rubahi
nazar aai na mujhe qafila-salaron men

vo shabani ki hai tamhid-e-kalimul-lahi
lazzat-e-naghma kahan murgh-e-khush-alhan ke liye

aah us baagh men karta hai nafas kotahi
ek sarmasti o hairat hai sarapa tarik

ek sarmasti o hairat hai tamam agahi
sifat-e-barq chamakta hai mira fikr-e-buland

ki bhaTakte na phiren zulmat-e-shab men raahi
