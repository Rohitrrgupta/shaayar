yuun haath nahin aata vo gauhar-e-yak-dana
yak-rangi o azadi ai himmat-e-mardana

ya sanjar o tughral ka ain-e-jahangiri
ya aql ki rubahi ya ishq-e-yadul-lahi

ya shar-e-musalmani ya dair ki darbani
ya nara-e-mastana kaaba ho ki but-khana

miiri men faqiri men shahi men ghulami men
kuchh kaam nahin banta be-jurat-e-rindana
