mujh men tera jamal tha kya tha

tere jaane pe ab ke kuchh na kaha
dil men Dar tha malal tha kya tha

barq ne mujh ko kar diya raushan
tera aks-e-jalal tha kya tha

ham tak aaya tu bahr-e-lutf-o-karam
tera vaqt-e-zaval tha kya tha

jis ne tah se mujhe uchhal diya
Dubne ka khayal tha kya tha

jis pe dil saare ahd bhuul gaya
bhulne ka saval tha kya tha

titliyan the ham aur qaza ke paas
surkh phulon ka jaal tha kya tha
