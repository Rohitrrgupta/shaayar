dua men zikr kyuun ho muddaa ka
ki ye sheva nahin ahl-e-raza ka

talab meri bahut kuchh hai magar kya
karam tera hai ik dariya ata ka

kahan tak naaz uThae akhir ai husn
havas tere mizaj-e-khud sata ka

nahin malum kya ai shah-e-khuban
tujhe kuchh haal apne mubtila ka

vazifa hai mira subh o masa ka
ghazab ka samna hai ashiqon ko

nisar un par hue achchhe rahe ham
taqaza tha yahi khu-e-vafa ka

bahut mushtaq hai arz-e-khata ka
tiri mahfil men ahl-e-dil ko jalva

nazar aa jaega shan-e-khuda ka
uThaya hai maza dil ne bahut kuchh

jafa ko bhi vafa samjho ki 'hasrat'
tumhen haq un se kya chun-o-chara ka
