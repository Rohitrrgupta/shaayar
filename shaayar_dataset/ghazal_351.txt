ye qissa vo nahin tum jis ko qissa-khvan se suno
mire fasana-e-gham ko miri zaban se suno

sunao dard-e-dil apna to dam-ba-dam fariyad
misal-e-nai miri har ek ustukhvan se suno

karo hazar sitam le ke zikr kya yak yaar
shikayat apni tum is apne nim-jan se suno

khuda ke vaste ai hamdamo na bolo tum
payam laaya hai kya nama-bar vahan se suno

tumhare ishq ne rusva kiya jahan men hamen
hamara zikr na tum kyunki ik jahan se suno

suno tum apni jo tegh-e-nigah ke ausaf
jo tum ko sunna ho us shokh-e-dil-sitan se suno

'zafar' vo bosa to kya dega par koi dushnam
jo tum ko sunna ho us shokh-e-dil-sitan se suno
