ab to be-dad pe be-dad karegi duniya
ham na honge to hamen yaad karegi duniya

zindagi bhaag chali maut ke darvaze se
ab qafas kaun sa ijad karegi duniya

ham to hazir hain par ai silsila-e-jaur-e-qadim
khatm kab virsa-e-ajdad karegi duniya

samne aenge apni hi vafa ke pahlu
jab kisi aur ko barbad karegi duniya

kya hue ham ki na the marg-e-bashar ke qaail
log puchhenge to fariyad karegi duniya
