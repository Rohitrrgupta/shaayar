ye baat bazm men raushan hui zabani-e-shama
kare hai sarf ba-ima-e-shoala qissa tamam

gham us ko hasrat-e-parvana ka hai ai shoale
tire larazne se zahir hai na-tavani-e-shama

tire khayal se ruuh ehtizaz karti hai
nashat-e-dagh-e-gham-e-ishq ki bahar na puchh

jale hai dekh ke balin-e-yar par mujh ko
na kyuun ho dil pe mire dagh-e-bad-gumani-e-shama
