qavi dil shadman dil parsa dil
tire ashiq ne bhi paaya hai kya dil

laga do aag uzr-e-maslahat ko
ki hai be-zar is shai se mira dil

laga kar aankh is jan-e-jahan se
na hoga ab kisi se ashna dil

tire gham ko na de kyunkar dua dil
na pahunchegi kabhi kya gosh-e-gul tak

qafas se uḌ ke fariyad-e-ana dil
tu ana-e-sadaqat hai to hargiz

na hoga pairav-e-batil mira dil
baḌi dargah ka saail huun 'hasrat'

baḌi ummid hai meri baḌa dil
