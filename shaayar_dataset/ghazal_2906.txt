aise hijr ke mausam kab kab aate hain
tere alava yaad hamen sab aate hain

jagti ankhon se bhi dekho duniya ko
khvabon ka kya hai vo har shab aate hain

jazb kare kyuun ret hamari ashkon ko
tera daman tar karne ab aate hain

ab vo safar ki taab nahin baaqi varna
ham ko bulave dasht se jab tab aate hain

kaghaz ki kashti men dariya paar kiya
dekho ham ko kya kya kartab aate hain
