zamana sakht kam-azar hai ba-jan-e-asad
vagarna ham to tavaqqo ziyada rakhte hain

tan-e-ba-band-e-havas dar nadada rakhte hain
tamiz-e-zishti-o-neki men laakh baten hain

ba-rang-e-saya hamen bandagi men hai taslim
ki dagh-e-dil ba-jabin-e-kushada rakhte hain

adab ne saunpi hamen surma-sai-e-hairat
