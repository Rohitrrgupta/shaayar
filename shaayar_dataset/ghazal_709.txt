al-qissa 'mir' ko ham be-ikhtiyar paaya
ahval khush unhon ka ham-bazm hain jo tere

afsos hai ki ham ne vaan ka na baar paaya
jiite jo zof ho kar zakhm-e-rasa se us ke

siine ko chaak dekha dil ko figar paaya
shahr-e-dil ek muddat ujḌa basa ghamon men

akhir ujaaḌ dena us ka qarar paaya
itna na tujh se milte ne dil ko kho ke rote

jaisa kiya tha ham ne vaisa hi yaar paaya
kya e'tibar yaan ka phir us ko khvar dekha

jis ne jahan men aa kar kuchh e'tibar paaya
aahon ke shole jis ja uThte the 'mir' se shab

vaan ja ke subh dekha musht-e-ghubar paaya
