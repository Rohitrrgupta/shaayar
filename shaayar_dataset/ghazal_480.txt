maabad-e-zist men but ki misal jaḌe honge
ye nanhe bachche jis roz baḌe honge

itne dukhi is darja udaas jo saae hain
raat ke dasht men tez hava se laḌe honge

dhuup ke qahr ki lazzat ke shaidai hain
ye ashjar bhi khvab se chaunk paḌe honge

ham ko khala ki vusat se fursat na mili
laakh khazane is dharti men gaḌe honge

vo din hoga akhiri din ham sab ke liye
aina dekhne jab ham log khaḌe honge
