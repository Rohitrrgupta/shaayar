pahle to mujhe kaha nikalo
phir bole gharib hai bula lo

be-dil rakhne se faeda kya
tum jaan se mujh ko maar Daalo

us ne bhi to dekhi hain ye ankhen
aankh arasi par samajh ke Daalo

aaya hai vo mah bujha bhi do shama
parvanon ko bazm se nikalo

ghabra ke ham aae the su-e-hashr
yaan pesh hai aur majra lo

takiye men gaya to main pukara
shab tiira hai jaago sone vaalo

aur din pe 'amir' takiya kab tak
tum bhi to kuchh aap ko sambhalo
