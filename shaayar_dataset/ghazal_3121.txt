gham nahin hota hai azadon ko besh az-yak-nafas
barq se karte hain raushan sham-e-matam-khana ham

mahfilen barham kare hai ganjifa-baz-e-khayal
zoaf se hai ne qanaat se ye tark-e-justuju

daim-ul-habs is men hain lakhon tamannaen 'asad'
jante hain sina-e-pur-khun ko zindan-khana ham

bas-ki hain bad-mast-e-ba-shikan ba-shikan-e-mai-khana ham
mu-e-shisha ko samajhte hain khat-e-paimana ham

bas-ki har-yak-mu-e-zulf-afshan se hai tar-e-shuaa
panja-e-khurshid ko samjhe hain dast-e-shana ham

fart-e-be-khvabi se hain shab-ha-e-hijr-e-yar men
hasrat-e-arz-e-tamanna yaan se samjha chahiye

do-jahan hashr-e-zaban-e-khushk hain juun shana ham
kashti-e-alam ba-tufan-e-taghaful de ki hain

nang-e-balidan hain juun mu-e-sar-e-divana ham
