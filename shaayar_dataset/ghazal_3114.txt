umr bhar us ne isi tarah lubhaya hai mujhe
vo jo is dasht ke us paar se laaya hai mujhe

kitne ainon men ik aks dikhaya hai mujhe
zindagi ne jo akela kabhi paaya hai mujhe

tu mira kufr bhi hai tu mira iman bhi hai
tu ne luuTa hai mujhe tu ne basaya hai mujhe

main tujhe yaad bhi karta huun to jal uThta huun
tu ne kis dard ke sahra men ganvaya hai mujhe

tu vo moti ki samundar men bhi shoala-zan tha
main vo aansu ki sar-e-khak giraya hai mujhe

itni khamosh hai shab log Dare jaate hain
aur main sochta huun kis ne bulaya hai mujhe

meri pahchan to mushkil thi magar yaron ne
zakhm apne jo kurede hain to paaya hai mujhe

vaiz-e-shahr ke naaron se to kya khulti aankh
khud mire khvab ki haibat ne jagaya hai mujhe

ai khuda ab tire firdaus pe mera haq hai
tu ne is daur ke dozakh men jalaya hai mujhe
