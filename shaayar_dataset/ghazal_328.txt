gulshan men bandobast ba-rang-e-digar hai aaj
qumri ka tauq halqa-e-bairun-e-dar hai aaj

aata hai ek para-e-dil har fughan ke saath
ai afiyat kinara kar ai intizam chal

sar-rishta chak-e-jeb ka tar-e-nazar hai aaj
nur-e-charagh-e-bazm se josh-e-sahar hai aaj

karti hai ajizi-e-safar sokhtan tamam
pairahan-e-khasak men ghubar-e-sharar hai aaj
