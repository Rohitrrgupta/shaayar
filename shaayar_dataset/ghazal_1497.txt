us ko kisi ke vaste be-tab dekhte
ham bhi kabhi ye manzar-e-nayab dekhte

sahil ki ret ne hamen vapas bula liya
barish ka lutf band makanon men kuchh nahin

bahar nikalte ghar se to sailab dekhte
aati kisi ko raas shahadat 'husain' ki

duniya men ham kisi ko to sairab dekhte
raton ko jagne ke siva aur kya kiya

ankhen agar mili thiin koi khvab dekhte
