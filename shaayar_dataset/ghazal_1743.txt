kali kahte hain us ka sa dahan hai
suna kariye ki ye bhi ik sukhan hai

Tapakte dard hain aansu ki jaaga
ilahi chashm ya zakhm-e-kuhan hai

khabar le pir-e-kanan ki ki kuchh aaj
nahin daman men laala be-sutun ke

ki har gul us men ik khunin-kafan hai
karun kya hasrat-e-gul ko vagarna

dil par daagh bhi apna chaman hai
jo de aram Tak avargi 'mir'

to sham-e-ghurbat ik subh-e-vatan hai
