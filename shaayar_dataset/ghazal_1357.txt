mukhtasar hote hue bhi zindagi baḌh jaegi
maan ki ankhen chuum liije raushni baḌh jaegi

maut ka aana to tai hai maut aaegi magar
aap ke aane se thoḌi zindagi baḌh jaegi

itni chahat se na dekha kijiye mahfil men aap
shahr valon se hamari dushmani baḌh jaegi

aap ke hansne se khatra aur bhi baḌh jaega
is tarah to aur ankhon ki nami baḌh jaegi

bevafai khel ka hissa hai jaane de ise
tazkira us se na kar sharmindagi baḌh jaegi
