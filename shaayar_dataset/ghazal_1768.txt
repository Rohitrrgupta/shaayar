betabiyon men tang ham aae hain jaan se
vaqt-e-shakeb khush ki gaya darmiyan se

kya kya liye gae tire ashiq jahan se
ham hamushon ka zikr tha shab us ki bazm men

nikla na harf-e-khair kasu ki zaban se
ab-e-khizar se bhi na gai sozish-e-jigar

kya janiye ye aag hai kis dudman se
juz ishq jang-e-dahr se mat paḌh ki khush hain ham

us qisse ki kitab men us dastan se
aane ka is chaman men sabab bekali hui

juun barq ham taḌap ke gire ashiyan se
ab chheḌ ye rakhi hai ki ashiq hai to kahen

al-qissa khush guzarti hai us bad-guman se
kiine ki mere tujh se na chahega koi daad

main kah marunga apne har ik mehrban se
daghon se hai chaman jigar-e-'mir' dahr men

un ne bhi gul chune bahut us gulistan se
