ishq ko be-naqab hona tha
aap apna javab hona tha

teri ankhon ka kuchh qusur nahin
haan mujhi ko kharab hona tha

aao mil jaao muskura ke gale
ho chuka jo itaab hona tha

jis ko khana-kharab hona tha
dil ki jis par hain naqsh-e-ranga-rang

us ko saada kitab hona tha
ham ne nakamiyon ko DhunD liya

haae vo lamha-e-sukun ki jise
kyuun na hota sitam bhi be-payan

kyuun nazar hairaton men Duub gai
ho chuka roz-e-avvalin hi 'jigar'

jis ko jitna kharab hona tha
