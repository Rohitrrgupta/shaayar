safar kaThin hi sahi jaan se guzarna kya
jo chal paḌe hain to ab raah men Thaharna kya

jo dil men gunjti ho aankh se jhalakti ho
kisi ke samne us baat se mukarna kya

inhi ravan davan lahron pe zindagi kaT jaae
ho tera saath mayassar to paar utarna kya

mile na gauhar-e-maqsud Duub kar bhi agar
to laash ban ke phir us bahr se ubharna kya

jis ab-rud ki auqat chand qatre ho
to us ko phandna kya us men paanv dharna kya

jahan ghurur hunar-parvari ho pumba-e-gosh
fasad-e-khalq bhi hangama didani tha 'zafar'

phir ek baar vahi shosha chhoḌ Darna kya
