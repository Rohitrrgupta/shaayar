dil men bhara za-bas-ki khayal-e-sharab tha
manind aaine ke mire ghar men aab tha

maujen kare hai bahr-e-jahan men abhi to tu
janega baad-e-marg ki aalam habab tha

ugte the dast-e-bulbul-o-daman gul baham
Tuk dekh ankhen khol ke us dam ki hasraten

jis dam ye sujhegi ki ye aalam bhi khvab tha
dil jo na tha to raat za-khud-raftagi men 'mir'

gah intizar o gaah mujhe iztirab tha
