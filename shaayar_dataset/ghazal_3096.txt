tera ghar aur mera jangal bhigta hai saath saath
aisi barsaten ki badal bhigta hai saath saath

bachpane ka saath hai phir ek se donon ke dukh
raat ka aur mera anchal bhigta hai saath saath

vo ajab duniya ki sab khanjar-ba-kaf phirte hain aur
kanch ke pyalon men sandal bhigta hai saath saath

barish-e-sang-e-malamat men bhi vo hamrah hai
main bhi bhigun khud bhi pagal bhigta hai saath saath

laḌkiyon ke dukh ajab hote hain sukh us se ajiib
hans rahi hain aur kajal bhigta hai saath saath

barishen jaaḌe ki aur tanha bahut mera kisan
jism aur iklauta kambal bhigta hai saath saath
