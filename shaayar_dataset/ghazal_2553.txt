miri nava se hue zinda aarif o aami
diya hai main ne unhen zauq-e-atish ashami

haram ke paas koi aajmi hai zamzama-sanj
ki taar taar hue jaama haae ehrami

badalte rahte hain andaz-e-kufi o shami
mujhe ye Dar hai muqamir hain pukhta-kar bahut

na rang laae kahin tere haath ki khami
ajab nahin ki musalman ko phir ata kar den

shikva-e-sanjar o faqr-e-juned o bastami
qaba-e-ilm o hunar lutf-e-khas hai varna

tiri nigah men thi meri na-khush andami
