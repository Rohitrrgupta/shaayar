ishq kya kya afaten laata raha
akhir ab duuri men ji jaata raha

mehr o mah gul phuul sab the par hamen
chehrai chehra hi vo bhata raha

dil hua kab ishq ki rah ka dalil
main to khud gum hi use paata raha

munh dikhata barson vo khush-ru nahin
chaah ka yuun kab talak naata raha

kuchh na main samjha junun o ishq men
der naseh mujh ko samjhata raha

daagh tha jo sar pe mere shama saan
paanv tak mujh ko vahi khata raha

kaise kaise ruk gae hain 'mir' ham
muddaton munh tak jigar aata raha
