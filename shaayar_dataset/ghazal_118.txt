shikast-e-dil men bhi ik zindagi nazar aai
diya bujha to hamen raushni nazar aai

faraz-e-dar pe koi hamen na pahchana
zamane valon ko khush-qamati nazar aai

sar-e-dayar-e-vafa doston ke jhurmuT men
darida jism liye dosti nazar aai

ab aur tarz se naqqara-e-khuda gunje
zaban-e-khalq to khamosh hi nazar aai

vo rakh-rakhav na dekha diye ke jalne men
jo us ke bujhne men shaistagi nazar aai
