har ik saans ko ham saba jante hain

lahu ro ke sincha hai ham ne chaman ko
har ik phuul ka majra jante hain

jise naghma-e-nai samajhti hai duniya
use bhi ham apni sada jante hain

ishara kare jo nai zindagi ka
ham us khud-kushi ko rava jante hain
