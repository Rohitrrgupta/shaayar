jab bhi ghar ki chhat par jaaen naaz dikhane aa jaate hain
kaise kaise log hamare ji ko jalane aa jaate hain

din bhar jo suraj ke Dar se galiyon men chhup rahte hain
shaam aate hi ankhon men vo rang purane aa jaate hain

jin logon ne un ki talab men sahraon ki dhuul uḌaai
ab ye hasin un ki qabron par phuul chaḌhane aa jaate hain

kaun sa vo jaadu hai jis se gham ki andheri sard gupha men
laakh nisai saans dilon ke rog miTane aa jaate hain

ze ke reshmi rumalon ko kis kis ki nazron se chhupaen
kaise hain vo log jinhen ye raaz chhupane aa jaate hain

ham bhi 'munir' ab duniya-dari kar ke vaqt guzarenge
hote hote jiine ke bhi laakh bahane aa jaate hain
