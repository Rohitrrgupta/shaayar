naala juz husn-e-talab ai sitam-ijad nahin
ham ko taslim niko-nami-e-farhad nahin

kam nahin vo bhi kharabi men pa vusat malum
dasht men hai mujhe vo aish ki ghar yaad nahin

ahl-e-binash ko hai tufan-e-havadis maktab
janta hai ki hamen taqat-e-faryad nahin

sabad-e-gul ke tale band kare hai gulchin
muzhda ai murgh ki gulzar men sayyad nahin

nafi se karti hai isbat taravish goya
di hai ja-e-dahn us ko dam-e-ijad nahin

kam nahin jalvagari men tire kuche se bahisht
yahi naqsha hai vale is qadar abad nahin

karte kis munh se ho ghurbat ki shikayat 'ghalib'
tum ko be-mehri-e-yaran-e-vatan yaad nahin
