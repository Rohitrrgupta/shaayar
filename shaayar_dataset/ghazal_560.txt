rangon ki vahshaton ka tamasha thi bam-e-sham
taari tha har makan pe jalal-e-davam-e-sham

aage ki manzilon ki taraf shaam ka safar
jaise shabon ke dil men tha shahr-e-qayam-e-sham

bandhe hue hain vaqt sabhi us ke hukm men
hai jis khuda ke haath men kar-e-nizam-e-sham

dhundla gai hai shaam shab-e-kham se 'munir'
khali hua kashish ki sharabon se jam-e-sham
