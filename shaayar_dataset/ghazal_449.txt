ek tujh ko hazar men dekha
jal gaya dil safed hain ankhen

ye to kuchh intizar men dekha
jaisa muztar tha zindagi men dil

vohin main ne qarar men dekha
aable ka bhi hona daman-gir

tere kuche ke khaar men dekha
tiira aalam hua ye roz-e-siyah

apne dil ke ghubar men dekha
zabh kar main kaha tha marta huun

dam nahin mujh shikar men dekha
jin balaon ko 'mir' sunte the

un ko is rozgar men dekha
