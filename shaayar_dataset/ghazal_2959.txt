karenge ahl-e-nazar taaza bastiyan abad
ye madrasa ye javan ye surur o raanai

inhin ke dam se hai mai-khana-e-farang abad
na falsafi se na mulla se hai gharaz mujh ko

ye dil ki maut vo andesha o nazar ka fasad
faqih-e-shahr ki tahqir kya majal miri

magar ye baat ki main DhunDta huun dil ki kushad
kharid sakte hain duniya men ishrat-e-parvez

khuda ki den hai sarmaya-e-gham-e-farhad
kiye hain faash rumuz-e-qalandari main ne

rishi ke faqon se TuuTa na barhaman ka tilism
asa na ho to kalimi hai kar-e-be-buniyad
