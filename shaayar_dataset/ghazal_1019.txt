jaan hi lene ki hikmat men taraqqi dekhi
maut ka rokne vaala koi paida na hua

koi hasrat mire dil men kabhi aai hi nahin
tha hi aisa ki ye maqbul-e-tamanna na hua

us ki beTi ne uTha rakkhi hai duniya sar par
khairiyat guzri ki angur ke beTa na hua

dil-farebi miri duniya ne to behad chahi
meri hi himmat-o-ghairat ka taqaza na hua
