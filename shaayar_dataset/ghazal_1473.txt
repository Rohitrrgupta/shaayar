ashk ankhon men kab nahin aata
lohu aata hai jab nahin aata

hosh jaata nahin raha lekin
jab vo aata hai tab nahin aata

so vo muddat se ab nahin aata
dil se rukhsat hui koi khvahish

girya kuchh be-sabab nahin aata
ishq ko hausla hai shart arna

baat ka kis ko Dhab nahin aata
ji men kya kya hai apne ai hamdam

par sukhan ta-ba-lab nahin aata
duur baiTha ghubar-e-'mir' us se

ishq bin ye adab nahin aata
