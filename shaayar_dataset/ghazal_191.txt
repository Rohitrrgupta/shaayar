hasad se dil agar afsurda hai garm-e-tamasha ho
ki chashm-e-tang shayad kasrat-e-nazzara se va ho

bharun yak-gosha-e-daman gar ab-e-haft-dariya ho
agar vo sarv-qad garm-e-khiram-e-naz aa jaave

baham balidan-e-sang-o-gul-e-sahra ye chahe hai
ki tar-e-jada bhi kohsar ko zunnar-e-mina ho

ki misl-e-ghuncha saz-e-yak-gulistan dil muhayya ho
bajae daana khirman yak-bayaban baiza-e-qumri

mira hasil vo nuskha hai ki jis se khaak paida ho
kare kya saz-e-binish vo shahid-e-dard-agahi

nigah labrez-e-ashk o siina maamur-e-tamanna ho
na dekhen ru-e-yak-dil sard ghair-az sham-e-kafuri

khudaya is qadar bazm-e-'asad' garm-e-tamasha ho
