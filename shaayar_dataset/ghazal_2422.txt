mere jism ke saare ilaqe jal-thal ho jaaen

honT-nadi sailab ka mujh pe darvaza khole
ham ko mayassar aise bhi ik do pal ho jaaen

dushman dhund hai kab se meri ankhon ke darpai
hijr ki lambi kaali raten kajal ho jaaen

umr ka lamba hissa kar ke danai ke naam
ham bhi ab ye soch rahe hain pagal ho jaaen
