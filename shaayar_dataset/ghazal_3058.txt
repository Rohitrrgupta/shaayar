din saliqe se uga raat Thikane se rahi
dosti apni bhi kuchh roz zamane se rahi

chand lamhon ko hi banti hain musavvir ankhen
zindagi roz to tasvir banane se rahi

is andhere men to Thokar hi ujala degi
raat jangal men koi shama jalane se rahi

fasla chand bana deta hai har patthar ko
duur ki raushni nazdik to aane se rahi

shahr men sab ko kahan milti hai rone ki jagah
apni izzat bhi yahan hansne hansane se rahi
