duzdida nigah karna phir aankh milana bhi
is lauTte daman ko paas aa ke uThana bhi

pamali-e-ashiq ko manzur rakhe jaana
phir chaal kaDhab chalna Thokar na lagana bhi

burqa ko uTha dena par aadhe hi chehre se
kya munh ko chhupana bhi kuchh jhamki dikhana bhi

dekh ankhen miri nichi ik marna patthar bhi
zahir men satana bhi parde men jatana bhi

sohbat hai ye vaisi hi ai jaan ki asaish
saath aan ke sona bhi phir munh ko chhupana bhi
