vo jin ke naqsh-e-qadam dekhne men aate hain
ab aise log to kam dekhne men aate hain

kahin nahin hai minara na mimbar o mehrab
mahal-sara se haram dekhne men aate hain

tavaf-e-ku-e-sukhan khatm hi nahin hota
koi nahin hai to ham dekhne men aate hain

aTe hue hain ghubar-e-shikastagi men 'salim'
jo aaine pas-e-gham dekhne men aate hain
