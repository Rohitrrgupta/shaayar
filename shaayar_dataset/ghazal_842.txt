jab chaha kar liya hai kunj-e-qafas baharan
ankhon men dard-mandi honTon pe uzr-khvahi

namus-e-jan-o-dil ki baazi lagi thi varna
asan na thi kuchh aisi rah-e-vafa-shiaran

mujrim ho khvah koi rahta hai nasehon ka
hai ab bhi vaqt zahid tarmim-e-zuhd kar le

shayad qarib pahunchi subh-e-visal hamdam
hai apni kisht-e-viran sarsabz is yaqin se

aenge is taraf bhi ik roz abr-o-baran
aaegi 'faiz' ik din bad-e-bahar le kar
