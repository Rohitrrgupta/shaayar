mashal-e-dard phir ek baar jala li jaae
jashn ho jaae zara dhuum macha li jaae

khuun men josh nahin aaya zamana guzra
dosto aao koi baat nikali jaae

jaan bhi meri chali jaae to kuchh baat nahin
vaar tera na magar ek bhi khali jaae

jo bhi milna hai tire dar hi se milna hai use
dar tira chhoḌ ke kaise ye savali jaae

vasl ki subh ke hone men hai kuchh der abhi
dastan hijr ki kuchh aur baḌha li jaae
