sab rang men us gul ki mire shaan hai maujud
ghafil tu zara dekh vo har aan hai maujud

har taar ka daman ke mire kar ke tabarruk
sar-basta har ik khar-e-bayaban hai maujud

ham ko ye tire ishq men saman hai maujud
kis tarah lagave koi daman ko tire haath

hone ko tu ab dast-o-gareban hai maujud
leta hi raha raat tire rukh ki balaen

tu puchh le ye zulf-e-pareshan hai maujud
tum chashm-e-haqiqat se agar aap ko dekho

aina-e-haq men dil-e-insan hai maujud
kahta hai 'zafar' hain ye sukhan aage sabhon ke

jo koi yahan sahib-e-irfan hai maujud
