jab se asir-e-zulf-e-girah-gir ho gaya
jaata kahan bhala tiri mahfil ko chhoḌ kar

main apne aap paanv ki zanjir ho gaya
nur-e-jahan koi na koi yuun to sab ki thi

daulat se ek shakhs jahangir ho gaya
mujh men rachi hui tiri khushbu thi is liye

baḌh kar 'adu bhi mujh se baghal-gir ho gaya
phir bandh li kisi se umid-e-vafa 'qatil'

phir ik mahal havaon men taamir ho gaya
