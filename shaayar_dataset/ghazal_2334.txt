ek main aur itne lakhon silsilon ke samne
ek saut-e-gung jaise gumbadon ke samne

miTte jaate naqsh dud-e-dam ki aamad raft se
khulte jaate be-sada lab ainon ke samne

hai hava-e-sair-e-ab aur ajnabi si sar-zamin
uḌ rahi hai khak-e-kohna sahilon ke samne

aag jalti hai gharon men ya koi tasvir hai
dushmani rasm-e-jahan hai dosti harf-e-ghalat

aadmi tanha khaḌa hai zalimon ke samne
chaar chup chizen hain bahr-o-bar falak aur kohsar

dil dahal jaata hai in khali jaghon ke samne
batin-e-zardar pur-asrar hai jaise 'munir'

kan-e-zar ki band haibat mashalon ke samne
