jada-e-rah khur ko vaqt-e-sham hai tar-e-shuaa
charkh va karta hai mah-e-nau se aghosh-e-vidaa

shama se hai bazm-e-angusht-e-tahayyur dar dahan
juun par-e-taus jauhar takhta mashq-e-rang hai

bas-ki hai vo qibla-e-aina mahv-e-ikhtiraa
chaar su-e-dahr men bazar-e-ghaflat garm hai

aql ke nuqsan se uThta hai khayal-e-intifaa
ashna 'ghalib' nahin hain dard-e-dil ke ashna

varna kis ko mere afsane ki tab-e-istimaa
