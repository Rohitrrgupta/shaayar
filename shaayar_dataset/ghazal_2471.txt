tere tausan ko saba bandhte hain
ham bhi mazmun ki hava bandhte hain

aah ka kis ne asar dekha hai
ham bhi ek apni hava bandhte hain

teri fursat ke muqabil ai umr
barq ko pa-ba-hina bandhte hain

ashk ko be-sar-o-pa bandhte hain
mast kab band-e-qaba bandhte hain

log naale ko rasa bandhte hain
ablon par bhi hina bandhte hain

saada purkar hain khuban 'ghalib'
ham se paiman-e-vafa bandhte hain

paanv men jab vo hina bandhte hain
mere hathon ko juda bandhte hain

shauq ko pa-ba-hina bandhte hain
qaid men bhi hai asiri azad

chashm-e-zanjir ko va bandhte hain
shaikh-ji kaabe ka jaana ma'lum

aap masjid men gadha bandhte hain
kis ka dil zulf se bhaga ki 'asad'

tere bimar pe hain fariyadi
vo jo kaghaz men dava bandhte hain
