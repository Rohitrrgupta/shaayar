mera kya tha main TuuTa ki bikhra raha
tere hathon men to ik khilauna raha

ik zara si ana ke liye 'umr bhar
tum bhi tanha rahe main bhi tanha raha

tere jaane ka manzar hi gham-khvar tha
zindagi bhar jo ankhon se lipTa raha

mera ehsas sadiyon pe phaila hua
aisa aansu jo palken badalta raha

ghar ki sab raunaqen mujh se aur main 'vasim'
taaq par ik diye jaisa jalta raha
