kaam ab koi na aaega bas ik dil ke siva
raste band hain sab kucha-e-qatil ke siva

ham-safar koi nahin duri-e-manzil ke siva
ham ne duniya ki har ik shai se uThaya dil ko

lekin ek shokh ke hangama-e-mahfil ke siva
tegh munsif ho jahan dar-o-rasan hon shahid

be-gunah kaun hai us shahr men qatil ke siva
jaane kis rang se aai hai gulistan men bahar

koi naghma hi nahin shor-e-salasil ke siva
