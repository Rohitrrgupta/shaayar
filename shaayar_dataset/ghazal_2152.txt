raushni dar raushni hai us taraf
zindagi dar zindagi hai us taraf

jin azabon se guzarte hain yahan
un azabon ki nafi hai us taraf

ik rihaish khvahish-e-dil ki tarah
ik numaish khvab ki hai us taraf

jo bikhar kar rah gaya hai is jagah
husn ki ik shakl bhi hai us taraf

justuju jis ki yahan par ki 'munir'
us se milne ki khushi hai us taraf
