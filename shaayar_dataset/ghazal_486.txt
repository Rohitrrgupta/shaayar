vo mulaqat thi ajab koi

jab tujhe pahli baar dekha tha
vo bhi tha mausam-e-tarab koi

duur baiTha hai jan-ba-lab koi

yaad aati hain duur ki baten

pyaar se dekhta hai jab koi
choT khaai hai bar-ha lekin

aaj to dard hai ajab koi
jin ko miTna tha miT chuke 'nasir'

un ko rusva kare na ab koi
