go maslak-e-taslim-o-raza bhi hai koi chiiz
par ghairat-arbab-e-vafa bhi hai koi chiiz

khulta hai har ik ghuncha-e-nau-josh-e-numu se
ye sach hai magar lams-e-hava bhi hai koi chiiz

ye be-rukhi-e-fitrat mahbub ke shaki
itna bhi na samjhe ki ada bhi hai koi chiiz

lazzat-kada-e-jurm-o-khata bhi hai koi chiiz
lapkega gareban pe to mahsus karoge

ai ahl-e-duval dast-e-gada bhi hai koi chiiz
