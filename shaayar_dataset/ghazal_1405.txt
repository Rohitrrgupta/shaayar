zulf dekhi vo dhuan-dhar vo chehra dekha
sach bata dekhne vaale use kaisa dekha

raat saari kisi TuuTi hui kashti men kaTi
aankh bistar pe khuli khvab men dariya dekha

zard galiyon men khule sabz dariche jin men
dhuup lipTi rahi aur saae ko jalta dekha

kaale kamron men kaTi saari javani us ki
jis ne ai subh-e-mohabbat tira rasta dekha

sunte rahte the ki yuun hoga vo aisa hoga
lekin us ko to kisi aur tarah ka dekha
