jis ki bahar ye ho phir us ki khizan na puchh
na-char bekasi ki bhi hasrat uThaiye

juz dil suragh-e-dard ba-dil-khuftagan na puchh
aina arz kar khat-o-khal-e-bayan na puchh

ya rab hisab-e-sakhti-e-khvab-e-giran na puchh
har dagh-e-taza yak-dil-e-dagh intizar hai

kahta tha kal vo mahram-e-raz apne se ki aah
tu mashq-e-baz kar dil-e-parvana hai bahar
