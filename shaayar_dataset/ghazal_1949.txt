ki mire dil men aa chhupa hai ishq

khel kaisa samajh liya hai ishq
talkh-kami pe ba-maza hai ishq

ham ko tarjih tum pe hai yaani
dilruba husn o jan-ruba hai ishq

dekh halat miri kahen kafir
naam dozakh ka kyuun dhara hai ishq

dekhiye kis jagah Dubo dega
meri kashti ka nakhuda hai ishq

ab to dil ishq ka maza chakkha
ham na kahte the kyuun bura hai ishq

aap mujh se nibahenge sach hai
ba-vafa husn o bevafa hai ishq

naam se mere bhagta hai ishq
qais o farhad o vamiq o 'momin'

mar gae sab hi kya vaba hai ishq
