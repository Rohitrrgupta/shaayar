vahi hain apni raton ki kathaen
vahi andar tamavvuj andhiyon ka

vahi bahar hava ki saaen saaen
yahan hai kaun aisa aane vaala

ki ham kamra saliqe se sajaen
koi in rat-jagon ki had bhi hogi

darichon men diye kab tak jalaen
kahan tak saath chal sakta hai koi

kahan tak saath dengi ye duaen
sulag uThta hai yuun be-sakhta dil

chamak uThti hain bahar ki fazaen
