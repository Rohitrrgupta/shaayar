ne mohra baaqi ne mohra-bazi
jiita hai 'rumi' haara hai 'razi'

raushan hai jam-e-jamshed ab tak
dil hai musalman mera na tera

tu bhi namazi main bhi namazi
main janta huun anjam us ka

jis marke men mulla hon ghazi
turki bhi shirin taazi bhi shirin

tu zindagi hai paendagi hai
baaqi hai jo kuchh sab khak-bazi
