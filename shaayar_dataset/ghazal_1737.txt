hujum-e-nala hairat ajiz-e-arz yak-afghan hai
khamoshi resha-e-sad-nistan se khas-ba-dandan hai

hui ye kasrat-e-gham se talaf kaifiyat-e-shadi
ki subh-e-id mujh ko bad-tar az-chak-e-gareban hai

dil o diin naqd la saaqi se gar sauda kiya chahe
ki us bazar men saghar mata-e-dast-gardan hai

gham aghosh-e-bala men parvarish deta hai ashiq ko
charagh-e-raushan apna qulzum-e-sarsar ka marjan hai

takalluf saz-e-rusvai hai ghafil sharm-e-ranai
dil-e-khun-gashta dar dast-e-hina-aluda uryan hai
