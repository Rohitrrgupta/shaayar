tiri gali bhi mujhe yuun to khinchti hai bahut

dar-asl hai miri miTTi kahan ki kya ma'lum
kahan se laun ilahi vo durbin ankhen

safar zarur hai aur uzr ki majal nahin
maza to ye hai na manzil na rasta ma'lum

na ibtida ki khabar hai na intiha ma'lum

talab karen bhi to kya shai talab karen ai 'shad'
hamin ko aap nahin apna muddaa ma'lum
