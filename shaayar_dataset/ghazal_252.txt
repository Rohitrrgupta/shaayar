phuul pe dhuul babul pe shabnam dekhne vaale dekhta ja
ab hai yahi insaf ka aalam dekhne vaale dekhta ja

parvanon ki raakh uḌa di bad-e-sahar ke jhonkon ne
shama bani hai paikar-e-matam dekhne vaale dekhta ja

jam-ba-jam lagi hain mohren mai-khanon par pahre hain
roti hai barsat chhama-chham dekhne vaale dekhta ja

is nagri ke raj-dulare ek tarah kab rahte hain
Dhalte saae badalte mausam dekhne vaale dekhta ja

maslahaton ki dhuul jami hai ukhḌe ukhḌe qadmon par
jhijak jhijak kar uḌte parcham dekhne vaale dekhta ja

ek purana madfan jis men dafn hain lakhon ummiden
chhalni chhalni sina-e-adam dekhne vaale dekhta ja

ek tira hi dil nahin ghayal dard ke maare aur bhi hain
kuchh apna kuchh duniya ka gham dekhne vaale dekhta ja

kam-nazron ki is duniya men der bhi hai andher bhi hai
pathraya har dida-e-pur-nam dekhne vaale dekhta ja
