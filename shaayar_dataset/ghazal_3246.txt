tere siva bhi koi mujhe yaad aane vaala tha
main varna yuun hijr se kab ghabrane vaala tha

jan-bujh kar samajh kar main ne bhula diya
har vo qissa jo dil ko bahlane vaala tha

mujh ko nadamat bas is par hai log bahut khush hain
is lamhe ko kho kar jo pachhtane vaala tha

ye to khair hui dariya ne rukh tabdil kiya
mera shahr bhi us ki zad men aane vaala tha

ik ik kar ke sab raste kitne sunsan hue
yaad aaya main lambe safar par jaane vaala tha
