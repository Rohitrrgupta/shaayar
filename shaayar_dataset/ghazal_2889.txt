aur charon taraf hai ghar darpesh

hai ye aalam ajiib aur yahan
do jahan se guzar gaya phir bhi

main raha khud ko umr bhar darpesh
ab main ku-e-abas shitab chalun

kai ik kaam hain udhar darpesh
us ke didar ki ummid kahan

jab ki hai diid ko nazar darpesh
ab miri jaan bach gai yaani

ek qatil ki hai sipar darpesh
kis tarah kuuch par kamar bandhun

ek rahzan ki hai kamar darpesh
khud-nigar ko hai khud-nigar darpesh
