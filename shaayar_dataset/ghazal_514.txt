ye shab ye khayal-o-khvab tere
kya phuul khile hain munh-andhere

shoale men hai ek rang tera
baaqi hain tamam rang mere

ankhon men chhupae phir raha huun
yadon ke bujhe hue savere

dete hain suragh fasl-e-gul ka
shakhon pe jale hue basere

manzil na mili to qafilon ne
raste men jama liye hain Dere

jangal men hui hai shaam ham ko
basti se chale the munh-andhere

phir ashk na tham sakenge mere
