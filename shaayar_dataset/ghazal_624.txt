makan men qaid-e-sada ki dahshat
makan se bahar khala ki dahshat

hava hai khauf-e-khuda se khali
hai is nagar men bala ki dahshat

ghira hua huun main har taraf se
hai aaine men hava ki dahshat

zamin pe har samt hadd-e-akhir
falak pe la-intiha ki dahshat

shajar ke saae men maut dekho
samar men us ke fana ki dahshat
