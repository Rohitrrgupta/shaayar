na koi zakhm laga hai na koi daagh paḌa hai
ye ghar bahar ki raton men be-charagh paḌa hai

ajab nahin yahi kaif-afrin ho sham-e-abad tak
jo taq-e-sina men ik khuun ka ayagh paḌa hai

kabhi rulaegi us yar-e-bevafa ki tamanna
jo zindagi hai to sad lamha-e-faragh paḌa hai

ye ek shakhcha-e-gham se itne phuul jhaḌe hain
kabhi qarib se guzro to ek baagh paḌa hai
