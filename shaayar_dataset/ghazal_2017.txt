koi ham se sikhe vafadariyan
hamari to guzri isi taur umr

yahi naala karna yahi zariyan
farishta jahan kaam karta na tha

miri aah ne barchhiyan mariyan
gaya jaan se ik jahan le ke shokh

na tujh se gaiin ye dil-azariyan
kahan tak ye taklif-e-ma la-yutaq

kiya dard-o-gham ne mujhe na-umid
ki majnun ko ye hi thiin bimariyan

tiri ashnai se hi had hui
bahut ki thiin duniya men ham yariyan

na bhaai hamari to qudrat nahin
khinchin 'mir' tujh se hi ye khvariyan
