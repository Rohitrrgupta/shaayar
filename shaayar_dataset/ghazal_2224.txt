har taraf satvat-e-arzang dikhai degi
rang barsenge zamin dang dikhai degi

barish-e-khun-e-shahidan se vo aaegi bahar
saari dharti hamen gul-rang dikhai degi

parbaton se nikal aenge thirakte paikar
raaz men rah na sakegi koi izhar ki lai

ik na ik surat-e-ahang dikhai degi
soch ko jurat-e-parvaz to mil lene do

ye zamin aur hamen tang dikhai degi
taaj kanTon ka sahi ek na ik din lekin

jab bhi parkhega koi pyaar ke meayar 'qatil'
saari duniya mire pasang dikhai degi
