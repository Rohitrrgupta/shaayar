dekha to tha yunhi kisi ghaflat-shiar ne
divana kar diya dil-e-be-ikhtiyar ne

ai aarzu ke dhundle kharabo javab do
phir kis ki yaad aai thi mujh ko pukarne

tujh ko khabar nahin magar ik sada-lauh ko
barbad kar diya tire do din ke pyaar ne

main aur tum se tark-e-mohabbat ki aarzu
divana kar diya hai gham-e-rozgar ne

ab ai dil-e-tabah tira kya khayal hai
ham to chale the kakul-e-giti sanvarne
