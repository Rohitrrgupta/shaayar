bas yahi na dard kuchh dil ka siva ho jaega

vo mire dil ki pareshani se afsurda ho kyuun
dil ka kya hai kal ko phir achchha bhala ho jaega

ghar se kuchh khvabon se milne ke liye nikle the ham
kya khabar thi zindagi se samna ho jaega

rone lagta huun mohabbat men to kahta hai koi
kya tire ashkon se ye jangal hara ho jaega

kaise aa sakti hai aisi dil-nashin duniya ko maut
kaun kahta hai ki ye sab kuchh fana ho jaega
