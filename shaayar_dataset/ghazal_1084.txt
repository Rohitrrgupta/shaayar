rah gai baat be-qarari ki
vaan shikayat hai dost-dari ki

zid se ham tira-rozgari ki
kyuun na Dar jaun dekh kar vo zulf

hai shab-e-hijr ki si tariki
yaas dekho ki ghair se kah di

bas ki hai yaar ki kamar ka khayal
sher ki sujhti hai bariki

tere abru ki yaad men ham ne
qatl-e-dushman ka hai irada use

ye saza apni jan-nisari ki
kya musalman hue ki ai 'momin'

hasil us but se sharmsari ki
