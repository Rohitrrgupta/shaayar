bahta aansu ek jhalak men kitne ruup dikhaega
aankh se ho kar gaal bhigo kar miTTi men mil jaega

bhulne vale! vaqt ke aivanon men kaun Thaharta hai
biiti shaam ke darvaze par kis ko bulane aaega

ankh-micholi khel raha hai ik badli se ik taara
phir badli ki yurish hogi phir taara chhup jaega

andhiyare ke ghor-nagar men ek kiran abad hui
kis ko khabar hai pahla jhonka kitne phuul khilaega

phir ik lamha aan ruka hai vaqt ke suune sahra men
pal bhar apni chhab dikhla kar lamhon men mil jaega
