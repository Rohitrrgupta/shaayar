hone ko yuun to shahr men apna makan tha
nafrat ka regzar magar darmiyan tha

lamhe ke TuTne ki sada sun raha tha main
jhapki jo aankh sar pe naya asman tha

kahne ko haath bandhe khaḌe the namaz men
puchho to dusri hi taraf apna dhyan tha

allah jaane kis pe akaḌta tha raat din
kuchh bhi nahin tha phir bhi baḌa bad-zaban tha

shole ugalte tiir baraste the charkh se
saaya tha paas men na koi saeban tha

sab se kiya hai vasl ka vaada alag alag
kal raat vo sabhi pe bahut mehrban tha

munh-phaT tha be-lagam tha rusva tha DhiiT tha
jaisa bhi tha vo dosto mahfil ki jaan tha
