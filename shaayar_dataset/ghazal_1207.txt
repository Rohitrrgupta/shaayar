hamen vaqf-e-gham sar-ba-sar dekh lete
vo tum kuchh na karte magar dekh lete

jo vaaiz tira rahguzar dekh lete
rasai kahan bazm-e-dushman men apni

ki ham bhi unhen ik nazar dekh lete
tamanna ko phir kuchh shikayat na rahti

jo tum bhuul kar bhi idhar dekh lete
na rahti khabar diin o duniya ki 'hasrat'

jo sote unhen be-khabar dekh lete
