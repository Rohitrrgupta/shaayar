munh ki baat sune har koi dil ke dard ko jaane kaun
avazon ke bazaron men khamoshi pahchane kaun

sadiyon sadiyon vahi tamasha rasta rasta lambi khoj
lekin jab ham mil jaate hain kho jaata hai jaane kaun

vo meri parchhain hai ya main us ka aina huun
mere hi ghar men rahta hai mujh jaisa hi jaane kaun

jaane kya kya bol raha tha sarhad pyaar kitaben khuun
kal meri nindon men chhup kar jaag raha tha jaane kaun

kiran kiran alsata suraj palak palak khulti ninden
dhime dhime bikhar raha hai zarra zarra jaane kaun
