dil par khuun hai yahan tujh ko guman hai shisha
shaikh kyuun mast hua hai to kahan hai shisha

shisha-bazi to tanik dekhne aa ankhon ki
har palak par mire ashkon se ravan hai shisha

rish-e-qazi ke sabab pamba-dahan hai shisha
manzil-e-masti ko pahunche hai unhen se aalam

nisha-e-mai-balad va sang-e-nishan hai shisha
darmiyan halqa-e-mastan ke shab us ki ja thi

daur-e-saghar men magar pir-e-mughan hai shisha
ja ke puchha jo main ye kar-gah-e-mina men

dil ki surat ka bhi ai shisha-garan hai shisha
kahne laage ki kidhar phirta hai bahka ai mast

har tarah ka jo tu dekhe hai ki yaan hai shisha
dil hi saare the pe ik vaqt men jo kar ke gudaz

shakl shishe ki banae hain kahan hai shisha
jhuk gaya dekh ke main 'mir' use majlis men

chashm-e-bad-dur trahdar javan hai shisha
