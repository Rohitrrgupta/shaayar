ada-e-tul-e-sukhan kya vo ikhtiyar kare
bahut hi talkh-nava huun magar aziz-e-vatan

main kya karun jo tira dard be-qarar kare
qadam ko faiz-e-junun se vo hausla hai nasib

jo khar-e-rah ko bhi sham-e-rahguzar kare
jagaen ham-safaron ko uThaen parcham-e-shauq

na jaane kab ho sahar kaun intizar kare
misal milti hai kitnon ki us divane se

chaman se duur jo baiTha gham-e-bahar kare
dayar-e-jaur men rasta hai ik yahi varna

kise pasand hai ai dil ki sair-dar kare
khuda kare gham-e-giti ka pech-o-tab ai dost

kuchh aur bhi tiri zulfon ko tabdar kare
sitam ki tegh-e-qalam den use jo ai 'majruh'

ghazal ko qatl kare naghme ko shikar kare
