tum apne shikve ki baten na khod khod ke puchho
hazar karo mire dil se ki is men aag dabi hai

dila ye dard-o-alam bhi to mughtanim hai ki akhir
na girya-e-sahari hai na ah-e-nim-shabi hai

ki khar-e-khushk ko bhi dava-e-chaman-nasabi hai
hua visal se shauq-e-dil-e-haris ziyada

lab-e-qadah pe kaf-e-bada josh-e-tishna-labi hai
khusha vo dil ki sarapa tilism-e-be-khabari ho

junun o yaas o alam rizq-e-muddaa-talabi hai
chaman men kis ke ye barham hui hai bazm-e-tamasha

ki barg barg-e-saman shisha reza-e-halabi hai
'ali' vali asadullah janashin-e-nabi hai
