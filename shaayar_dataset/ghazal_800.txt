yak-zarra-e-zamin nahin be-kar baagh ka
yaan jaada bhi fatila hai laale ke daagh ka

khincha hai ijz-e-hausla ne khat ayagh ka
bulbul ke karobar pe hain khanda-ha-e-gul

kahte hain jis ko ishq khalal hai dimagh ka
taaza nahin hai nashsha-e-fikr-e-sukhan mujhe

sau baar band-e-ishq se azad ham hue
par kya karen ki dil hi adu hai faragh ka

be-khun-e-dil hai chashm men mauj-e-nigah ghubar
ye mai-kada kharab hai mai ke suragh ka

abr-e-bahar khum-kada kis ke dimagh kaa
hai abr pamba rauzan-e-divar-e-bagh ka
