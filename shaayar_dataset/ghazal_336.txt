par apne jaam men tujh bin lahu tha
ghalat tha aap se ghafil guzarna

na samjhe ham ki is qalib men tu tha
chaman ki vaza ne ham ko kya daagh

ki har ghuncha-dil par aarzu tha
jidhar dekha tidhar tera hi ru tha

karoge yaad baten to kahoge
jahan par hai fasane se hamare

dimagh-e-ishq ham ko bhi kabhu tha
magar divana tha gul bhi kaso ka

ki pairahan men so jaaga rafu tha
kahin kya baal tere khul gae the

ki jhonka baav ka kuchh mushkbu tha
na dekha 'mir' avara ko lekin

ghubar ik na-tavan sa ku-ba-ku tha
