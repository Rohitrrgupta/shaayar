kya barabar ka mohabbat men asar hota hai
dil idhar hota hai zalim na udhar hota hai

ham ne kya kuchh na kiya dida-e-dil ki khatir
log kahte hain duaon men asar hota hai

dil to yuun dil se milaya ki na rakkha mera
ab nazar ke liye kya hukm-e-nazar hota hai

main gunahgar-e-junun main ne ye maana lekin
kuchh udhar se bhi taqaza-e-nazar hota hai

kaun dekhe use betab-e-mohabbat ai dil
tu vo naale hi na kar jin men asar hota hai
