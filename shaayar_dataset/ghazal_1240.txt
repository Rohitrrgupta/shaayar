gar khamushi se faeda ikhfa-e-hal hai
khush huun ki meri baat samajhni muhal hai

kis ko sunaun hasrat-e-izhar ka gila
kis parde men hai aina-pardaz ai khuda

hai hai khuda-na-khvasta vo aur dushmani
ai shauq-e-munfail ye tujhe kya khayal hai

mushkin libas-e-kaba ali ke qadam se jaan
naf-e-zamin hai na ki naf-e-ghazal hai

vahshat pe meri arsa-e-afaq tang tha
dariya zamin ko araq-e-infial hai

hasti ke mat fareb men aa jaiyo 'asad'
pahlu-tahi na kar gham-o-andoh se 'asad'

dil vaqf-e-dard kar ki faqiron ka maal hai
