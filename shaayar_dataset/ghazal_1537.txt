vaade ki jo saaat dam-e-kushtan hai hamara
jo dost hamara hai so dushman hai hamara

ye kah-e-ruba se bhi hain kam ai kashish-e-dil
mazkur kuchh aisa pas-e-chilman hai hamara

jo qahqaha shadi hai so shevan hai hamara
mahtab ka kya rang kiya dud-e-fughan ne

ahval shab-e-tar se raushan hai hamara
deta nahin us zof pe bhi josh-e-junun chain

har reg-e-ravan dasht men tausan hai hamara
tafrih na kyunkar ho hava aa nahin sakti

goya dar o divar nasheman hai hamara
gar paas hai logon ka to aa ja ki qalaq se

hai laash kahin aur kahin madfan hai hamara
jazb-e-dil use khinch ke laae to kahan laae

jo ghair ka ghar hai vahi maskan hai hamara
but-khane se kaabe ko chale rashk ke maare

'momin' khizr-e-rah barhaman hai hamara
