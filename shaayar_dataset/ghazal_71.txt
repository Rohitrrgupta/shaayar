tilism khatm chalo ah-e-be-asar ka hua
vo dekho jism barahna har ik shajar ka hua

sunaun kaise ki suraj ki zad men hain sab log
jo haal raat ko parchhaiyon ke ghar ka hua

sada ke saae men sannaTon ko panah mili
ajab ki shahr men charcha na is khabar ka hua

khala ki dhund hi ankhon pe mehrban rahi
harif koi ufuq kab miri nazar ka hua

main sochta huun magar yaad kuchh nahin aata
ki ikhtitam kahan khvab ke safar ka hua
