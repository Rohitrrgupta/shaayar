juz qais aur koi na aaya ba-ru-e-kar
ashuftagi ne naqsh-e-suvaida kiya durust

zahir hua ki daagh ka sarmaya duud tha
tha khvab men khayal ko tujh se muamla

jab aankh khul gai na ziyan tha na suud tha
leta huun maktab-e-gham-e-dil men sabaq hanuz

lekin yahi ki raft gaya aur buud tha
main varna har libas men nang-e-vajud tha

teshe baghair mar na saka kohkan 'asad'
juun subh chak-e-jeb mujhe tar-o-pud tha

bazi-khur-e-fareb hai ahl-e-nazar ka zauq
tangi rafiq-e-rah thi adam ya vajud tha

tu yak-jahan qamash-e-havas jama kar ki main
gardish-muhit-e-zulm raha jis qadar falak

puchha tha garche yaar ne ahval-e-dil magar
khur shabnam-ashna na hua varna main 'asad'
