yuun be-kar na baiTho din bhar yuun paiham aansu na bahao
itna yaad karo ki bil-akhir asani se bhuul bhi jaao

saare raaz samjho lo lekin khud kyuun un ko lab par laao
dhoka dene vaala ro de aisi shaan se dhoka khaao

zulmat se manus hain ankhen chand ubhra to mund jaengi
balon ko uljha rahne do ik uljhav sau suljhav

kal ko kal par rakkho jab kal aaega dekha jaega
aaj ki raat bahut bhari hai aaj ki raat yahin rah jaao

kab tak yuun parde men husn mohabbat ko Thukrata
maut ka din bhi hashr ka din hai chhupne vaalo samne aao
