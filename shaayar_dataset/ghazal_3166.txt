na koi hijr na koi visal hai shayad
bas ek halat-e-be-mah-o-sal hai shayad

hua hai dair-o-haram men jo moatakif vo yaqin
khayal-o-vahm se bartar hai us ki zaat so vo

main sath-e-harf pe tujh ko utaar laaya huun
tira zaval hi mera kamal hai shayad

main ek lamha-e-maujud se idhar na udhar
so jo bhi mere liye hai muhal hai shayad

vo inhimak har ik kaam men ki khatm na ho
to koi baat hui hai malal hai shayad

guman hua hai ye amboh se javabon ke
saval khud hi javab-e-saval hai shayad
