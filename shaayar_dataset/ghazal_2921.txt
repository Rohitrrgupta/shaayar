kisi ne bhi to na dekha nigah bhar ke mujhe
gaya phir aaj ka din bhi udaas kar ke mujhe

saba bhi laai na koi payam apnon ka
suna rahi hai fasane idhar udhar ke mujhe

muaaf kiije jo main ajnabi huun mahfil men
ki raste nahin maalum is nagar ke mujhe

vo dard hai ki jise sah sakun na kah paun
milega chain to ab jaan se guzar ke mujhe
