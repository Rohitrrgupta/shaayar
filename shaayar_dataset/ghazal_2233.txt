mahram nahin hai tu hi nava-ha-e-raz ka
yaan varna jo hijab hai parda hai saaz ka

ye vaqt hai shaguftan-e-gul-ha-e-naz ka
tu aur su-e-ghair nazar-ha-e-tez tez

main aur dukh tiri mizha-ha-e-daraz ka
sarfa hai zabt-e-ah men mera vagarna men

toama huun ek hi nafas-e-jan-gudaz ka
hain bas-ki josh-e-bada se shishe uchhal rahe

har gosha-e-bisat hai sar shisha-baz ka
kavish ka dil kare hai taqaza ki hai hunuz

nakhun pe qarz is girah-e-nim-baz ka
siina ki tha dafina gohar-ha-e-raz ka
