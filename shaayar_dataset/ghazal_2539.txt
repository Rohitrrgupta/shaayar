dast-e-munim miri mehnat ka kharidar sahi
koi din aur main rusva sar-e-bazar sahi

main tira dam-e-khizan laakh giraftar sahi
jast karta huun to laḌ jaati hai manzil se nazar

hail-e-rah koi aur bhi divar sahi
ghairat-e-sang hai saaqi ye gulu-e-tishna

tere paimane men jo mauj hai talvar sahi
main ne dekhi dekhi usi men gham-e-dauran ki jhalak

be-khabar rang-e-jahan se nigah-e-yar sahi
un se bichhḌe hue 'majruh' zamana guzra

ab bhi honTon men vahi garmi-e-rukhsar sahi
