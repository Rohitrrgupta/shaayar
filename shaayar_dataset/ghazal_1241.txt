yunhi mar mar ke jien vaqt guzare jaaen
zindagi ham tire hathon se na maare jaaen

ab zamin par koi gautam na mohammad na masih
asmanon se nae log utare jaaen

vo jo maujud nahin us ki madad chahte hain
vo jo sunta hi nahin us ko pukare jaaen

baap larzan hai ki pahunchi nahin barat ab tak
aur ham-joliyan dulhan ko sanvare jaaen

ham ki nadan juari hain sabhi jante hain
dil ki baazi ho to ji jaan se haare jaaen

taj diya tum ne dar-e-yar bhi ukta ke 'faraz'
ab kahan DhunDhne gham-khvar tumhare jaaen
