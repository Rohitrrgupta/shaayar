log rukhsat hue kab yaad nahin
avvalin qurb ki sarshari men

kitne arman the jo ab yaad nahin
dil men har vaqt chubhan rahti thi

thi mujhe kis ki talab yaad nahin
vo sitara thi ki shabnam thi ki phuul

ek surat thi ajab yaad nahin
jab se vo aariz o lab yaad nahin

bhulte jaate hain maazi ke dayar
yaad aaen bhi to sab yaad nahin

aisa uljha huun gham-e-duniya men
ek bhi khvab-e-tarab yaad nahin

rishta-e-jan tha kabhi jis ka khayal
us ki surat bhi to ab yaad nahin

ye haqiqat hai ki ahbab ko ham
yaad hi kab the jo ab yaad nahin

dil ke bujhne ka sabab yaad nahin
