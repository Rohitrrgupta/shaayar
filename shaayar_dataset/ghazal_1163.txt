zakhm-e-ummid bhar gaya kab ka
qais to apne ghar gaya kab ka

ab to munh apna mat dikhao mujhe
naseho main sudhar gaya kab ka

aap ab puchhne ko aae hain
dil miri jaan mar gaya kab ka

aap ik aur niind le liije
qafila kuuch kar gaya kab ka

mera fihrist se nikal do naam
main to khud se mukar gaya kab ka
