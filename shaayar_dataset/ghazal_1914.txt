chandi jaisa rang hai tera sone jaise baal
ik tu hi dhanvan hai gori baaqi sab kangal

har angan men aae tere ujle ruup ki dhuup
chhail-chhabeli raani thoḌa ghunghaT aur nikal

bhar bhar nazren dekhen tujh ko ate-jate log
dekh tujhe badnam na kar de ye hirni si chaal

kitni sundar naar ho koi main avaz na duun
tujh sa jis ka naam nahin hai vo ji ka janjal

samne tu aae to dhaḌken mil kar lakhon dil
ab jaana dharti par kaise aate hain bhaunchal

beach men rang-mahal hai tera khaai charon or
ham se milne ki ab gori tu hi raah nikal

kar sakte hain chaah tiri ab 'sarmad' ya 'mansur'
mile kisi ko daar yahan aur khinche kisi ki khaal

ye duniya hai khud-gharazon ki lekin yaar 'qatil'
tu ne hamara saath diya to jiye hazaron saal
