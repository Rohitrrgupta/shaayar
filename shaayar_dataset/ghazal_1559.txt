kanTa tha chashm-e-yas men ek ek rang-e-gul
mere liye chaman bhi bayaban nikal gaya

dast-e-junun ka zof se uThna muhal tha
kya jaane kis tarah se gareban nikal gaya

dil men to aag hi vahi ab tak lagi hui
maana ki chashm-e-shauq ka arman nikal gaya

josh-e-junun se kuchh na chali zabt-e-ishq ki
sau sau jagah se aaj gareban nikal gaya
