khoven hain niind meri musibat bayaniyan
tum bhi to ek raat suno ye kahaniyan

kya aag de ke taur ko ki tark sar-kashi
us sho'le ki vahi hain shararat ki baniyan

sohbat rakha kiya vo safina-o-zalal se
dil hi men khuun hua kiin miri nukta-daniyan

ham se to kiine hi ki adaen chali gaiin
talvar ke tale hi gaya ahd-e-imbisat

mar mar ke ham ne kaaTi hain apni javaniyan
gaali sivae mujh se sukhan mat kiya karo

achchhi lage hain mujh ko tiri bad-zabaniyan
ghairon hi ke sukhan ki taraf gosh-e-yar the

is harf-e-na-shino ne hamari na maniyan
ye be-qarariyan na kabhu in nai-dekhiyan

jan-kahiyan hamari bahut sahl janiyan
maara mujhe bhi saan ke ghairon men un ne 'mir'

kya khaak men milain miri jan-fishaniyan
