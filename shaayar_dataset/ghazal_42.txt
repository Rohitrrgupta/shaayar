kis buri saaat se khat le kar gaya
nama-bar ab tak na aaya mar gaya

jaate hi dil us gali men mar gaya
marne vaala bevafai kar gaya

dil to jaane ko gaya lekin mujhe
us bhari mahfil men rusva kar gaya

hasraten thiin jiine vaali ji gaiin
marne vaala tha dil apna mar gaya

gham ki lazzat ibtida men thi magar
is qadar khaya ki ab ji bhar gaya

har nivala us ka ab to talkh hai
umr nemat thi magar ji bhar gaya

kya kahun ahval uThti bazm ka
pahle miina baad ko saghar gaya

dil ne ik din bhi na dekha tujh ko aah
us gali tak khvab men aksar gaya

marne vaale dil tujhe ab kya kahun
khair bakhsha main ne jo kuchh kar gaya

kya kahun in ansuon ka zor o shor
ankhen jhapki thiin ki chullu bhar gaya

dil gila karta tha khuub us shokh ka
tazkira mahshar ka sun kar Dar gaya

jis jagah daara ko bheja tha vahin
kuchh dinon ke baad askandar gaya

jis ka kahlata hai vaan thi kya kami
ai gada kyuun mangne dar-dar gaya

dil ne sabqat ki havas o sabr par
pahle sultan baad ko lashkar gaya

munh se nikla tha ki pahuncha arsh par
nala-e-dil naam uncha kar gaya
