ab haal apna us ke hai dil-khvah
mar jaao koi parva nahin hai

kitna hai maghrur allah allah
kahte hain us ke tu munh lagega

ho yuun hi yarab juun hai ye afvah
hazrat se us ki jaana kahan hai

ab mar rahega yaan banda dargah
sab aql khoe hai rah-e-mohabbat

ho khizr dil men kaisa hi gumrah
mujrim hue ham dil de ke varna

kis ko kaso se hoti nahin chaah
kya kya na rijhen tum ne pachain

achchha rijhaya ai mehrban aah
guzre hai dekhen kyunkar hamari

us bevafa se ne rasm ne raah
gardan men us ki har gah-o-be-gah

us par ki tha vo shah-e-rag se aqrab
hargiz na pahuncha ye dast-e-kotah

hai ma-siva kya jo 'mir' kahiye
agah saare us se hain agah

jalve hain us ke shanen hain us ki
kya roz kya khor kya raat kya maah

zahir ki batin avval ki akhir
