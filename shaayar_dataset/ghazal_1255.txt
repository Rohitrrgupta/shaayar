har gali shahr ki yaan kucha-e-rusvai tha

itni guzri jo tire hijr men so us ke sabab
sabr marhum ajab monis-e-tanhai tha

tere jalve ka magar ru tha sahar gulshan men
nargis ik dida-e-hairan tamashai tha

yahi zulfon ki tiri baat thi ya kakul ki
'mir' ko khuub kiya sair to saudai tha
