tu vo bad-khu ki tahayyur ko tamasha jaane
gham vo afsana ki ashufta-bayani mange

vo tap-e-ishq-e-tamanna hai ki phir surat-e-shama
tishna-e-khun-e-tamasha jo vo paani mange

rang ne gul se dam-e-arz-e-pareshani-e-bazm
barg-e-gul reza-e-mina ki nishani mange

amad-e-khat se na kar khanda-e-shirin ki mabad
khvab sayyad se parvaz-e-girani mange

chashm parvaz o nafas khufta magar zoaf-e-umid
vahshat-e-shor-e-tamasha hai ki juun nikhat-e-gul

gar mile hazrat-e-'bedil' ka khat-e-lauh-e-mazar
rang ne gul se dam-e-arz-e-pareshani-e-bazm

barg-e-gul reza-e-mina ki nishani mange
