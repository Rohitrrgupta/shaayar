takhta-e-lala ki har sham-e-farozan jaane
kis bhulave men mujhe dekh ke lahrai hai

khak-dar-khak chhupi hai miri ankhon ki chamak
jis kharabe men tiri anjuman-arai hai

apne hi paanv ki avaz se Dar jaata huun
main huun aur rah-guzar-e-besha-e-tanhai hai

phir sar-e-subh kisi dard ke dar va karne
dhaan ke khet se ik mauj-e-hava aai hai
