be-vajh naiin hai aaina har baar dekhna
koi dam ko phulta hai ye gulzar dekhna

nargis ki tarah khaak meri ugen hain chashm
Tuk aan ke ye hasrat-e-didar dekhna

khinche to tegh hai haram-e-dil ke said par
ai ishq par bhala tu mujhe maar dekhna

hai naqs-e-jan diid tira par yahi hai dhun
ji jaao yaar ho mujhe yak-bar dekhna

ai tifl-e-ashk hai falak-e-haftamin pa arsh
aage qadam na rakhiyo tu zinhar dekhna

puchhe khuda sabab jo mire ishtiyaq ka
meri zaban se ho yahi izhar dekhna

har naqsh-e-pa pe taḌpe hai yaaro har ek dil
Tuk vaste khuda ke ye raftar dekhna

karta to hai tu aan ke 'sauda' se ikhtilat
koi lahr aa gai to mire yaar dekhna

tujh bin ajab maash hai 'sauda' ka in dinon
tu bhi Tuk us ko ja ke sitamgar dekhna

ne harf o ne hikayat o ne sher o ne sukhan
ne sair-e-bagh o ne gul-o-gulzar dekhna

khamosh apne kalba-e-ahzan men roz o shab
tanha paḌe hue dar-o-divar dekhna

ya ja ke us gali men jahan tha tira guzar
le subh ta-ba-sham kai baar dekhna

taskin-e-dil na is men bhi paai to bahr-e-shaghl
paḌhna ye sher gar kabhu ashar dekhna

kahte the ham na dekh saken roz-e-hijr ko
par jo khuda dikhae so na-char dekhna
