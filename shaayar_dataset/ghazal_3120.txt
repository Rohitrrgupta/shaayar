raha na halqa-e-sufi men soz-e-mushtaqi
fughan ki takht o musalla kamal-e-razzaqi

karegi davar-e-mahshar ko sharmsar ik roz
na chini o arabi vo na ruumi o shami

sama saka na do aalam men mard-e-afaqi
mai-e-shabana ki masti to ho chuki lekin

khaTak raha hai dilon men karishma-e-saqi
chaman men talkh-navai miri gavara kar

ki zahr bhi kabhi karta hai kar-e-tiryaqi
vo sher jis men ho bijli ka soz-o-barraqi
