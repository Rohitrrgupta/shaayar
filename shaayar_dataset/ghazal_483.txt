chak-pairahani-e-gul ko saba janti hai
masti-e-shauq kahan band-e-qaba janti hai

ham to badnam-e-mohabbat the so rusva Thahre
nasehon ko bhi magar khalq-e-khuda janti hai

kaun taqon pe raha kaun sar-e-rahguzar
shahr ke saare charaghon ko hava janti hai

havas inaam samajhti hai karam ko tere
aur mohabbat hai ki ehsan ko saza janti hai
