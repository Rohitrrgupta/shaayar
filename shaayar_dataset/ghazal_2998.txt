dahr men naqsh-e-vafa vajh-e-tasalli na hua
hai ye vo lafz ki sharminda-e-maani na hua

sabza-e-khat se tira kakul-e-sarkash na daba
ye zamurrad bhi harif-e-dam-e-afi na hua

main ne chaha tha ki andoh-e-vafa se chhuTun
vo sitamgar mire marne pe bhi raazi na hua

gar nafas jada-e-sar-manzil-e-taqvi na hua
huun tire vaada na karne men bhi raazi ki kabhi

kis se mahrumi-e-qismat ki shikayat kiije
ham ne chaha tha ki mar jaaen so vo bhi na hua

mar gaya sadma-e-yak-jumbish-e-lab se 'ghalib'
na-tavani se harif-e-dam-e-isi na hua

na hui ham se raqam hairat-e-khatt-e-rukh-e-yar
vusat-e-rahmat-e-haq dekh ki bakhsha jaave

mujh sa kafir ki jo mamnun-e-maasi na hua
