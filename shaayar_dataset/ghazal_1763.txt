tire dard se jis ko nisbat nahin hai
vo rahat musibat hai rahat nahin hai

junun-e-mohabbat ka divana huun main
mire sar men sauda-e-hikmat nahin hai

tire gham ki duniya men ai jan-e-alam
koi ruuh mahrum-e-rahat nahin hai

mujhe garm-e-nazzara dekha to hans kar
vo bole ki is ki ijazat nahin hai

jhuki hai tire bar-e-irfan se gardan
hamen sar uThane ki fursat nahin hai

ye hai un ke ik ru-e-rangin ka partav
tire sarfaroshon men hai kaun aisa

jise dil se shauq-e-shahadat nahin hai
taghaful ka shikva karun un se kyunkar

vo kah denge tu be-muravvat nahin hai
vo kahte hain shokhi se ham dilruba hain

hamen dil navazi ki aadat nahin hai
shahidan-e-gham hain subuk ruuh kya kya

ki us dil pe bar-e-nadamat nahin hai
