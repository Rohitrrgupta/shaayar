is ishq-e-khush-anjam ka aghaz to dekho

chashmak miri vahshat pe hai kya hazrat-e-naseh
arbab-e-havas haar ke bhi jaan pe khele

majlis men mire zikr ke aate hi uThe vo
badnami-e-ushshaq ka eazaz to dekho

mahfil men tum aghyar ko duz-dida nazar se
manzur hai pinhan na rahe raaz to dekho

us ghairat-e-nahid ki har taan hai dipak
shoala sa lapak jaae hai avaz to dekho

den paki-e-daman ki gavahi mire aansu
us yusuf-e-bedard ka eajaz to dekho
