dekh dariya ko ki tughyani men hai
tu bhi mere saath ab paani men hai

nuur ye us akhiri bose ka hai
chand sa kya teri peshani men hai

main bhi jaldi men huun kaise baat ho
tu bhi lagta hai pareshani men hai

muddai suraj ka saara shahr hai
raat ye kis ki nigahbani men hai

saare manzar ek se lagne lage
kaun shamil meri hairani men hai
