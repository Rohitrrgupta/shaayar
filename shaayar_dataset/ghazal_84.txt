jab raat gae tiri yaad aai sau tarah se ji ko bahlaya
kabhi apne hi dil se baten kiin kabhi teri yaad ko samjhaya

yunhi vaqt ganvaya moti sa yunhi umr ganvai sona si
sach kahte ho tum ham-sukhano is ishq men ham ne kya paaya

jab pahle-pahal tujhe dekha tha dil kitne zor se dhaḌka tha
vo lahr na phir dil men jaagi vo vaqt na lauT ke phir aaya

phir aaj tire darvaze par baḌi der ke baad gaya tha magar
ik baat achanak yaad aai main bahar hi se lauT aaya
