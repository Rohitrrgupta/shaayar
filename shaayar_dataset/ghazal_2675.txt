shab-e-firaq ko jab muzhda-e-sahar aaya
to ik zamana tira muntazir nazar aaya

tamam-umr ki sahra-navardiyon ke baad
ye kaun abla-pa is taraf se guzra hai

nuqush-e-pa men jo phulon ke rang bhar aaya
kise majal ki nazzara-e-jamal kare

is anjuman men jo aaya ba-chashm-e-tar aaya
tiri talab ke ghane jangalon men aag lagi

mire khayal men jab vahm-e-rahguzar aaya
simaT gaya miri bahon men jab vo paikar-e-rang

to is ka rang mujhe duur tak nazar aaya
is arizon men ki zid kibriya ki puuri ho

'nadim' khaak pe aflak se utar aaya
