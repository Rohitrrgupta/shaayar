meri taqdir muafiq na thi tadbir ke saath
khul gai aankh nigahban ki bhi zanjir ke saath

ho gae shaikh bhi hazir nai tafsir ke saath
na-tavani miri dekhi to musavvir ne kaha

Dar hai tum bhi kahin khinch aao na tasvir ke saath
sai-e-bazu ki yahan shart na thi tiir ke saath

lahza lahza hai taraqqi pe tira husn-o-jamal
jis ko shak ho tujhe dekhe tiri tasvir ke saath

baad sayyad ke main collage ka karun kya darshan
ab mohabbat na rahi is but-e-be-pir ke saath

main huun kya chiiz jo us tarz pe jaun 'akbar'
'nasikh' o 'zauq' bhi jab chal na sake 'mir' ke saath
