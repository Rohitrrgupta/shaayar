vahi meri kam-nasibi vahi teri be-niyazi
mire kaam kuchh na aaya ye kamal-e-nai-navazi

main kahan huun tu kahan hai ye makan ki la-makan hai
ye jahan mira jahan hai ki tiri karishma-sazi

isi kashmakash men guzrin miri zindagi ki raten
vo fareb-khurda shahin ki pala ho kargason men

use kya khabar ki kya hai rah-o-rasm-e-shahbazi
na zaban koi ghazal ki na zaban se ba-khabar main

koi dil-kusha sada ho ajami ho ya ki taazi
nahin faqr o saltanat men koi imtiyaz aisa

ye sipah ki tegh-bazi vo nigah ki tegh-bazi
koi karvan se TuuTa koi bad-guman haram se

ki amir-e-karvan men nahin khu-e-dil-navazi
