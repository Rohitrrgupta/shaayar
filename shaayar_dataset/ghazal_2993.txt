gar tujh ko hai yaqin-e-ijabat dua na maang
aata hai dagh-e-hasrat-e-dil ka shumar yaad

mujh se mire gunah ka hisab ai khuda na maang
ai aarzu shahid-e-vafa khun-baha na maang

juz bahr-e-dast-o-bazu-e-qatil dua na maang
kashana bas-ki tang hai ghafil hava na maang

dushman samajh vale nigah-e-ashna na maang
sar par vabal-e-saya-e-bal-e-huma na maang

yaani dua ba-juz kham-e-zulf-e-dota na maang
iisa tilism husn taghaful hai zinhar

juz pusht chashm nuskha arz dava na maang
aina dekh jauhar-e-barg-e-hina na maang
