na ham rahe na vo khvabon ki zindagi hi rahi
guman guman si mahak khud ko DhunDhti hi rahi

ajab tarah rukh-e-aindagi ka rang uḌa
dayar-e-zat men az-khud guzashtagi hi rahi

harim-e-shauq ka aalam bataen kya tum ko
harim-e-shauq men bas shauq ki kami hi rahi

pas-e-nigah-e-taghaful thi ik nigah ki thi
jo dil ke chehara-e-hasrat ki tazgi hi rahi

thi us men ek udasi ki jo saji hi rahi
badal gaya sabhi kuchh us dayar-e-budash men

gali thi jo tiri jaan vo tiri gali hi rahi
tamam dil ke mohalle ujaḌ chuke the magar

bahut dinon to hansi hi rahi khushi hi rahi
vo dastan tumhen ab bhi yaad hai ki nahin

jo khuun thukne valon ki be-hisi hi rahi
tiri kami hi rahi aur miri kami hi rahi
