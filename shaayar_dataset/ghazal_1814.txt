zabt kar ke hansi ko bhuul gaya
main to us zakhm hi ko bhuul gaya

ajnabi ajnabi ko bhuul gaya
subh tak vajh-e-jan-kani thi jo baat

main use shaam hi ko bhuul gaya
sab dalilen to mujh ko yaad rahin

bahs kya thi usi ko bhuul gaya
kyuun na ho naaz is zehanat par

ek main har kisi ko bhuul gaya
sab se pur-amn vaqia ye hai

aadmi aadmi ko bhuul gaya
har gham-e-zindagi ko bhuul gaya

khvab-ha-khvab jis ko chaha tha
rang-ha-rang usi ko bhuul gaya

kya qayamat hui agar ik shakhs
apni khush-qismati ko bhuul gaya
