aina kyuun na duun ki tamasha kahen jise
aisa kahan se laun ki tujh sa kahen jise

hasrat ne la rakha tiri bazm-e-khayal men
phunka hai kis ne gosh-e-mohabbat men ai khuda

sar par hujum-e-dard-e-gharibi se Daliye
vo ek musht-e-khak ki sahra kahen jise

hai chashm-e-tar men hasrat-e-didar se nihan
shauq-e-inan gusekhta dariya kahen jise

'ghalib' bura na maan jo vaaiz bura kahe
aisa bhi koi hai ki sab achchha kahen jise

ya rab hamen to khvab men bhi mat dikhaiyo
ye mahshar-e-khayal ki duniya kahen jise

hai intizar se sharar abad rustakhez
kis fursat-e-visal pe hai gul ko andalib
