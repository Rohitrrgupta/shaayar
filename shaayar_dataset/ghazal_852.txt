shab-e-vasl kya mukhtasar ho gai
zara aankh jhapki sahar ho gai

nigahon ne sab raz-e-dil kah diya
unhen aaj apni khabar ho gai

ye tarkib agar kargar ho gai
ilahi bura ho gham-e-ishq ka

suna hai ki un ko khabar ho gai
kiye mujh pe ehsan gham-e-yar ne

hamesha ko nichi nazar ho gai
bas ab dastan mukhtasar ho gai
