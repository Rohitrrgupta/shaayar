vahshaten baḌhti gaiin hijr ke azar ke saath
ab to ham baat bhi karte nahin gham-khvar ke saath

ham ne ik umr basar ki hai gham-e-yar ke saath
'mir' do din na jiye hijr ke azar ke saath

ab to ham ghar se nikalte hain to rakh dete hain
taaq par izzat-e-sadat bhi dastar ke saath

is qadar khauf hai ab shahr ki galiyon men ki log
chaap sunte hain to lag jaate hain divar ke saath

ek to khvab liye phirte ho galiyon galiyon
us pe takrar bhi karte ho kharidar ke saath

shahr ka shahr hi naseh ho to kya kijiyega
varna ham rind to bhiḌ jaate hain do-char ke saath

ham ko us shahr men taamir ka sauda hai jahan
log meamar ko chun dete hain divar ke saath

jo sharaf ham ko mila kucha-e-janan se 'faraz'
su-e-maqtal bhi gae hain usi pindar ke saath
