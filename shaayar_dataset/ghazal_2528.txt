kya kare meri masihai bhi karne vaala
zakhm hi ye mujhe lagta nahin bharne vaala

zindagi se kisi samjhaute ke ba-vasf ab tak
yaad aata hai koi marne marne vaala

us ko bhi ham tire kuche men guzar aae hain
zindagi men vo jo lamha tha sanvarne vaala

us ka andaz-e-sukhan sab se juda tha shayad
baat lagti hui lahja vo mukarne vaala

shaam hone ko hai aur aankh men ik khvab nahin
koi is ghar men nahin raushni karne vaala

dastaras men hain anasir ke irade kis ke
so bikhar ke hi raha koi bikharne vaala

isi ummid pe har shaam bujhae hain charagh
ek taara hai sar-e-bam ubharne vaala
