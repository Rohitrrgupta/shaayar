laghar itna huun ki gar tu bazm men ja de mujhe
mera zimma dekh kar gar koi batla de mujhe

kya taajjub hai jo us ko dekh kar aa jaae rahm
vaan talak koi kisi hiile se pahuncha de mujhe

munh na dikhlave na dikhla par ba-andaz-e-itab
khol kar parda zara ankhen hi dikhla de mujhe

yaan talak meri giraftari se vo khush hai ki main
zulf gar ban jaun to shane men uljha de mujhe
