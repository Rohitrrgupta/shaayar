maani ko bhula deti hai surat hai to ye hai
nature bhi sabaq siikh le zinat hai to ye hai

kamre men jo hansti hui aai mis-e-raana
teacher ne kaha ilm ki aafat hai to ye hai

ye baat to achchhi hai ki ulfat ho mison se
huur un ko samajhte hain qayamat hai to ye hai

pechida masail ke liye jaate hain england
zulfon men ulajh aate hain shamat hai to ye hai

public men zara haath mila lijiye mujh se
sahab mire iman ki qimat hai to ye hai
