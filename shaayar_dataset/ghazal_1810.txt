ye aankh kyuun hai ye haath kya hai
ye din hai kya chiiz raat kya hai

ye un ka aur mera saath kya hai
guman hai kya is sanam-kade par

fughan hai kis ke liye dilon men
falak hai kyuun qaid-e-mustaqil men

zamin pe harf-e-najat kya hai
hai kaun kis ke liye pareshan

pata to de asal baat kya hai
hai lams kyuun raegan hamesha

fana men khauf-e-sabat kya hai
tira ye sehar-e-nashat kya hai
