chhiḌa hai raag bhaunre ka hava ki hai nai dhun bhi
ghazab hai saal ke barah mahinon men ye phagun bhi

ishara karti hai fitrat idhar aa dekh bhi sun bhi
baḌe darshan tumhare ho gae raaja ki seva se

magar man ka panapna chahte ho to karo pun bhi
hue raushan ye ma'ni chand kyon sha'ir ko pyara hai

kamal is men ye hai aariz bhi hai abru bhi nakhun bhi
