gungunati si koi raat bhi aa jaati hai
aap aate hain to barsat bhi aa jaati hai

ham ko har-chand gavara nahin aansu lekin
apni jholi men ye khairat bhi aa jaati hai

arzuon ke janaze hi nahin palkon par
bijliyon ki kabhi barat bhi aa jaati hai

gardish-e-jam se haT kar bhi tiri ankhon se
vajd men gardish-e-halat bhi aa jaati hai

vo fasane jo mire naam se mansub hue
un fasanon men tiri baat bhi aa jaati hai
