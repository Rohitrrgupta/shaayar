khirad ne mujh ko ata ki nazar hakimana
sikhai ishq ne mujh ko hadis-e-rindana

na baada hai na surahi na daur-e-paimana
faqat nigah se rangin hai bazm-e-janana

miri nava-e-pareshan ko shairi na samajh
kali ko dekh ki hai tishna-e-nasim-e-sahar

isi men hai mire dil ka tamam afsana
koi batae mujhe ye ghayab hai ki huzur

sab ashna hain yahan ek main huun begana
farang men koi din aur bhi Thahar jaun

mire junun ko sambhale agar ye virana
maqam-e-aql se asan guzar gaya 'iqbal'

maqam-e-shauq men khoya gaya vo farzana
