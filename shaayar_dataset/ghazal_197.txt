subh ki aaj jo rangat hai vo pahle to na thi
kya khabar aaj khiraman sar-e-gulzar hai kaun

shaam gulnar hui jaati hai dekho to sahi
ye jo nikla hai liye mishal-e-rukhsar hai kaun

raat mahki hui aai hai kahin se puchho
aaj bikhrae hue zulf-e-trah-dar hai kaun

phir dar-e-dil pe koi dene laga hai dastak
janiye phir dil-e-vahshi ka talabgar hai kaun
