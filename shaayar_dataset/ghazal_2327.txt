ai dost koi mujh sa rusva na hua hoga
dushman ke bhi dushman par aisa na hua hoga

ab ashk-e-hinai se jo tar na kare mizhgan
vo tujh kaf-e-rangin ka maara na hua hoga

Tak gor-e-ghariban ki kar sair ki duniya men
un zulm-rasidon par kya kya na hua hoga

imroz kabhi apna farda na hua hoga
hai qaeda kulli ye ku-e-mohabbat men

dil gham jo hua hoga paida na hua hoga
is kohna-kharabe men abadi na kar munim

yak shahr nahin yaan jo sahra na hua hoga
ankhon se tiri ham ko hai chashm ki ab hove

jo fitna ki duniya men barpa na hua hoga
juz martaba-e-kal ko hasil kare hai akhir

yak qatra na dekha jo dariya na hua hoga
sad nashtar-e-mizhgan ke lagne se na nikla khuun

aage tujhe 'mir' aisa sauda na hua hoga
