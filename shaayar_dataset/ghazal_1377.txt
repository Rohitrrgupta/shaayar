agar kabhi tire azar se nikalta huun
to apne daera-e-kar se nikalta huun

hava-e-taza huun rukna nahin kahin bhi mujhe
gharon men ghusta huun ashjar se nikalta huun

kabhi hai us ke mazafat men numud miri
kabhi main apne hi asar se nikalta huun

main ghar men jab nahin hota to ghaas ki surat
daricha o dar-o-divar se nikalta huun

ghurub hota huun us paar se nikalta huun
vidaa karti hai rozana zindagi mujh ko

main roz maut ke manjdhar se nikalta huun
ruka hua koi sailab huun tabiat ka

hamesha tundi-e-raftar se nikalta huun
ise bhi kuchh miri himmat hi janiye jo kabhi

khayal-o-khvab ke ambar se nikalta huun
libas bechta huun ja ke pahle apna 'zafar'

to kuchh kharid ke bazar se nikalta huun
