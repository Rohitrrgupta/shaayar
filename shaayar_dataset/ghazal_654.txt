kya kahen apni us ki shab ki baat
kahiye hove jo kuchh bhi Dhab ki baat

ab to chup lag gai hai hairat se
phir khulegi zaban jab ki baat

baat vo hai jo hove ab ki baat
kis ka ru-e-sukhan nahin hai udhar

hai nazar men hamari sab ki baat
zulm hai qahr hai qayamat hai

ghusse men us ke zer-e-lab ki baat
kahte hain aage tha buton men rahm

hai khuda janiye ye kab ki baat
go ki atish-zaban the aage 'mir'

ab ki kahiye gai vo tab ki baat
