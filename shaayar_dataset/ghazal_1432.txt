sahl yuun rah-e-zindagi ki hai
har qadam ham ne 'ashiqi ki hai

ham ne dil men saja liye gulshan
jab baharon ne be-rukhi ki hai

zahr se dho liye hain honT apne
lutf-e-saqi ne jab kami ki hai

tere kuche men badshahi ki
jab se nikle gadagari ki hai

bas vahi surkh-ru hua jis ne
bahr-e-khun men shanavari ki hai

jo guzarte the 'dagh' par sadme
ab vahi kaifiyat sabhi ki hai
