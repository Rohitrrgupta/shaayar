ye baat alag hai mira qatil bhi vahi tha
is shahr men tarif ke qabil bhi vahi tha

asan tha bahut us ke liye harf-e-muravvat
aur marhala apne liye mushkil bhi vahi tha

tabir thi apni bhi vahi khvab-e-safar ki
ik haath men talvar thi ik haath men kashkol

zalim to vo tha hi mira saail bhi vahi tha
ham aap ke apne hain vo kahta raha mujh se

akhir saf-e-aghyar men shamil bhi vahi tha
main lauT ke aaya to gulistan-e-havas men

tha gul bhi vahi shor-e-anadil bhi vahi tha
daave the 'zafar' us ko bahut ba-khabari ke

dekha to mire haal se ghafil bhi vahi tha
