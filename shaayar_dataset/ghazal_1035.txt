ab harf-e-tamanna ko samaat na milegi
bechoge agar khvab to qimat na milegi

tashhir ke bazar men ai taaza kharidar
zebaishen mil jaengi qamat na milegi

lamhon ke taaqub men guzar jaengi sadiyan
yuun vaqt to mil jaega mohlat na milegi

socha hi na tha yuun bhi use yaad rakhenge
jab us ko bhulane ki bhi fursat na milegi

ta-umr vahi kar-e-ziyan ishq raha yaad
halanki ye maalum tha ujrat na milegi

taabir nazar aane lagi khvab ki surat
ab khvab hi dekhoge basharat na milegi

aina-sifat vaqt tira husn hain ham log
kal aaine tarsenge to surat na milegi
