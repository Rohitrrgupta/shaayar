laakh khurshed sar-e-bam agar hain to rahen
ham koi mom nahin hain ki pighal jaenge

har gali-kuche men rusva hue jin ki khatir
kya khabar thi ki vahi log badal jaenge

un ke pichhe na chalo un ki tamanna na karo
saae phir saae hain kuchh der men Dhal jaenge

qafile nindon ke aae hain unhen Thahra lo
varna ye duur bahut duur nikal jaenge
