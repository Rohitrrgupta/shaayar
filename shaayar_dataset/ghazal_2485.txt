laḌkhaḌate hue bhi aur sambhalte hue bhi
us ke dar par hi gae khvab men chalte hue bhi

ishq asar thi har rahguzar us ki thi
ham bhaTak sakte na the raah badalte hue bhi

ishq hai ishq ba-har-hal numu paega
yaani jalte hue bhi aur pighalte hue bhi

zindagi tere faqat ek tabassum ke liye
ham ki hanste hi rahe dard men Dhalte hue bhi

ek muddat se hain ham ishq men avara ba-kar
yaani ik umr hui phulte-phalte hue bhi

silsila tujh se hi tha tere talab-garon ka
barf hote hue bhi aag men jalte hue bhi

bin tire aisa andhera tha mire andar yaar
Dar laga ishq ke suraj ko nikalte hue bhi
