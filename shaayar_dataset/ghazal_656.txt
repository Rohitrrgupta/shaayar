tu abhi rahguzar men hai qaid-e-maqam se guzar
misr o hijaz se guzar paras o shaam se guzar

jis ka amal hai be-gharaz us ki jaza kuchh aur hai
huur o khiyam se guzar bada-o-jam se guzar

garche hai dil-kusha bahut husn-e-farang ki bahar
koh-shigaf teri zarb tujh se kushad-e-sharq-o-gharb

tegh-e-hilal ki tarah aish-e-niyam se guzar
tera imaam be-huzur teri namaz be-surur

aisi namaz se guzar aise imaam se guzar
