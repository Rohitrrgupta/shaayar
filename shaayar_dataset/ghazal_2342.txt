sair kar andalib ka ahval
hain pareshan chaman men kuchh par-o-bal

tab-e-gham to gai tabib vale
phir na aaya kabhu mizaj-e-bahal

sar uThaya ki ho gaya pamal
kyuun na dekhun chaman ko hasrat se

ashiyan tha mira bhi yaan par-sal
sard-mehri ki bas-ki gul-ru ne

oḌhi abr-e-bahar ne bhi shaal
hijr ki shab ko yaan taiin taḌpa

ki hua subh hote mera visal
ham to sah guzre kaj-ravi teri

na nibhe par ai falak ye chaal
dida-e-tar pe shab rakha tha 'mir'
