dhuup ankhon tak aa pahunchi hai raat guzar gai janan

bhor samay tak jis ne hamen baham uljhae rakkha
vo albeli resham aisi baat guzar gai janan

sada ki dekhi raat hamen is baar mili to chupke se
khali baat pe rakh ke kya saughat guzar gai janan

kis konpal ki aas men ab tak vaise hi sarsabz ho tum
ab to dhuup ka mausam hai barsat guzar gai janan

log na jaane kin raton ki muraden manga karte hain
apni raat to vo jo tere saat guzar gai janan

ab to faqat sayyad ki dildari ka bahana hai varna
ham ko daam men laane vaali ghaat guzar gai janan
