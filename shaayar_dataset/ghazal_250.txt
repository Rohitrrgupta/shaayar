payambaron ki tarah se jiyo zamane men

payam-e-shauq bano daulat-e-hunar ki tarah
ye zindagi bhi koi zindagi hai ham-nafaso

samundaron ke talatum ne mujh ko paala hai

chamak raha huun isi vaste guhar ki tarah
tamam koh o tal o bahr o bar hain zer-e-nagin

khula hua huun main shahin ke bal-o-par ki tarah
tamam daulat-e-kaunain hai khiraj us ka

ye dil nahin kisi luuTe hue nagar ki tarah

main shakh-e-vaqt men aaya huun ik samar ki tarah

na misl-e-shahd huun shirin na main shakar ki tarah
khizan ke dast-e-sitam ne mujhe chhua hai magar

tamam shoala o shabnam huun kashmar ki tarah
miri nava men hai lutf-o-surur-e-subh-e-nashat

har ek sher hai rindon ki sham-e-tar ki tarah
ye fatehana ghazal asr-e-nau ka hai ahang
