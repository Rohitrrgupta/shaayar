ham apne aap ko ik masala bana na sake
isi liye to kisi ki nazar men aa na sake

ham ansuon ki tarah vaste nibha na sake
rahe jin ankhon men un men hi ghar bana na sake

phir andhiyon ne sikhaya vahan safar ka hunar
jahan charagh hamen rasta dikha na sake

jo pesh pesh the basti bachane valon men
lagi jab aag to apna bhi ghar bacha na sake

mire khuda kisi aisi jagah use rakhna
jahan koi mire baare men kuchh bata na sake

tamam umr ki koshish ka bas yahi hasil
kisi ko apne mutabiq koi bana na sake

tasalliyon pe bahut din jiya nahin jaata
kuchh aisa ho ke tira eatibar aa na sake
