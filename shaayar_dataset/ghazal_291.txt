gard ko kuduraton ki dho na paae ham
dil bahut udaas hai ki ro na paae ham

vajud ke chahar samt regzar tha
kahin bhi khvahishon ke biij bo na paae ham

ruuh se to pahle din hi haar maan li
bojh apne jism ka bhi Dho na paae ham

dasht men to ek ham the aur kuchh na tha
shahr ke hujum men bhi kho na paae ham

ek khvab dekhne ki aarzu rahi
isi liye tamam 'umr so na paae ham
