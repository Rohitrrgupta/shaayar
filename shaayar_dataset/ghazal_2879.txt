bhaTak gaya ki manzilon ka vo suragh pa gaya
hamare vaste khala men rasta bana gaya

surahi dil ki ansuon ki os se bhari rahi
isi liye azab-e-hijr us ko raas aa gaya

sarab ka tilism TuTna bahut bura hua
ki aaj tishnagi ka e'tibar bhi chala gaya

tamam umr khvab dekhne men munhamik raha
aur is tarah haqiqaton ko vahima bana gaya
