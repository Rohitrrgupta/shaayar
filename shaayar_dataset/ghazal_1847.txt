sauda tha bala-e-josh par raat
bistar par bichhae neshtar raat

bigḌe the yahan vo aan kar raat
be-taur bani thi jaan par raat

ham ta-sahar aap men nahin the
kya jaane rahe vo kis ke ghar raat

afsana samajh ke so gae vo
aine men ho na mom jaadu

sote nahin ab vo ta-sahar raat
taare ankhen jhapak rahe the

tha baam pe kaun jalva-gar raat
andher paḌa zamane men haae

ne din ko hai mehr ne qamar raat
hai roz se siyah-tar raat

kya puchho ho munkar-o-nakir aah
bigḌe jo vo tan-e-ghair par raat

ye baat baḌhi ki mar gae ham
maut aai thi qissa mukhtasar raat

is ghar men hai aish-e-khuld 'momin'
kya jaane kahan hai din kidhar raat
