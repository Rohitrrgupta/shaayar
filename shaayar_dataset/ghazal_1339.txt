luun vaam bakht-e-khufta se yak-khvab-e-khush vale
'ghalib' ye khauf hai ki kahan se ada karun

khush vahshate ki arz-e-junun-e-fana karun
juun gard-e-rah jama-e-hasti qaba karun

aa ai bahar-e-naz ki tere khiram se
juun jaada gard-e-rah se nigah surma-sa karun

sabr aur ye ada ki dil aave asir-e-chak
dard aur ye kamin ki rah-e-nala va karun

vo be-dimagh-e-minnat-e-iqbal huun ki main
vo iltimas-e-lazzat-e-be-dad huun ki main

vo raz-e-nala huun ki ba-sharh-e-nigah-e-ijz
afshan ghubar-e-surma se fard-e-sada karun
