taza-dam jism-o-jan the furqat men

vasl us ka niDhal chhoḌ gaya
ahd-e-mazi jo tha ajab pur-hal

ek viran haal chhoḌ gaya
jhala-bari ke marhalon ka safar

dil ko ab ye bhi yaad ho ki na ho
kaun tha kya malal chhoḌ gaya

main bhi ab khud se huun javab-talab
vo mujhe be-saval chhoḌ gaya
