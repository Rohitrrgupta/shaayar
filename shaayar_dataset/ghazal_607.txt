kis pe qaabu jo tujhi pe nahin qaabu apna
kis se ummid hamen jab na hua tu apna

jam-e-mai dekh ke jaata raha qaabu apna
laḌkhaḌata huun pakaḌ le koi baazu apna

nikhat-e-gul bahut itrati hui phirti hai
vo kahin khol bhi den turra-e-gesu apna

us ne phir kar bhi na dekha ki ye hai kaun bala
ham ko tha zom ki chal jaega jaadu apna

jis ko samjhe ho vahi chiiz nahin muddat se
ham dikha denge kabhi chiir ke pahlu apna

par-e-parvaz nikalne de qafas men sayyad
kaam dega yahi TuuTa hua baazu apna

mil gae khaak men dar par tire itna baiThe
is riyazat pe bhi ab tak na hua tu apna

gham nahin jam-e-tilakar uTha rakh saaqi
kya gharaz ham ko salamat rahe chullu apna

chaukḌi bhuul ke munh takte hain mujh vahshi ke
aake is paanv pe sar rakhte hain aahu apna

kaun ai tul-e-shab-e-gham tira jhagḌa rakkhe
aaj qissa hi kiye dete hain yaksu apna

nikhat-e-khuld-e-barin phail gai koson tak
vo naha kar jo sukhane lage gesu apna

lillahil-hamd kudurat nahin rahne paati
munh dhula deta hai har subh ko aansu apna

shadi o gham ke rahenge yahi ragḌe jhagḌe
qissa jis vaqt talak hoga na yaksu apna

gham men parvana-e-marhum ke thamte nahin ashk
shama ai shama zara dekh to munh tu apna

be-adab mushaf-e-rukhsar pe jhuk paḌte hain
rakhiye shanon pe zara gher ke gesu apna
