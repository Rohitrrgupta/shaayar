jo ab bhi na taklif farmaiyega
to bas haath malte hi rah jaiyega

nigahon se chhup kar kahan jaiyega
mira jab bura haal sun paiyega

miTa kar hamen aap pachhtaiyega
nahin khel naseh junun ki haqiqat

hamen bhi ye ab dekhna hai ki ham par
kahan tak tavajjoh na farmaiyega

sitam ishq men aap asan na samjhen
ye dil hai ise dil hi bas rahne diije

kahin chup rahi hai zaban-e-mohabbat
magar shart ye hai na yaad aiyega

hamen bhi na ab chain aaega jab tak
in ankhon men aansu na bhar laiyega

zara phir to irshad farmaiyega
hamin jab na honge to kya rang-e-mahfil

kise dekh kar aap sharmaiyega
ye maana ki de kar hamen ranj-e-furqat

mohabbat mohabbat hi rahti hai lekin
kahan tak tabiat ko behlaiyega
