maghz rindon ka kha gaya vaaiz

tere kahne se rind jaenge
ye to hai khana-e-khuda vaaiz

allah allah ye kibr aur ye ghurur
kya khuda ka hai dusra vaaiz

hashr hone de dekhna vaaiz
ham hain qaht-e-sharab se bimar

kis maraz ki hai tu dava vaaiz
rah chuka mai-kade men saari umr

kabhi mai-khane men bhi aa vaaiz
hajv-e-mai kar raha tha mimbar par

ham jo pahunche to pi gaya vaaiz
dukht-e-raz ko bura mire aage

phir na kahna kabhi suna vaaiz
aaj karta huun vasf-e-mai main 'amir'

dekhun kahta hai is men kya vaaiz
