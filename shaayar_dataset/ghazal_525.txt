inqalab ek khvab hai so hai
dil ki duniya kharab hai so hai

bahar ik iztirab hai so hai
tar hai dasht us ke aks-e-manzar se

aur khud vo sarab hai so hai
jo bhi dasht-e-talab ka hai pas-e-ru

vahi zarrin-rikab hai so hai
shaikh sahab liye phiren tegha

barhaman fath-yab hai sau hai
dahr ashob hai savalon ka

aur khuda la-javab hai so hai
raushni ek khvab hai so hai
