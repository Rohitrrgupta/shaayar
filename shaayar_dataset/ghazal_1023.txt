junun ki dast-giri kis se ho gar ho na uryani
gareban-chak ka haq ho gaya hai meri gardan par

hazar aina dil bandhe hai bal-e-yak-tapidan par
falak se ham ko aish-e-rafta ka kya kya taqaza hai

mata-e-burda ko samjhe hue hain qarz rahzan par
ham aur vo be-sabab ranj-ashna dushman ki rakhta hai

shua-e-mehr se tohmat nigah ki chashm-e-rauzan par
fana ko saunp gar mushtaq hai apni haqiqat ka

farogh-e-tala-e-khashak hai mauquf gulkhan par
'asad' bismil hai kis andaz ka qatil se kahta hai

ki mashq-e-naz kar khun-e-do-alam meri gardan par
fusun-e-yak-dili hai lazzat-e-bedad dushman par

ki vajd-e-barq juun parvana bal-afshan hai khirman par
ki rishta bandhta hai pairahan angusht-e-sozan par

ye kya vahshat hai ai divane pesh-az-marg vavaila
