ye mausam-e-gul garche tarab-khez bahut hai
khush davat-e-yaran bhi hai yalghar-e-adu bhi

kya kijiye dil ka jo kam-amez bahut hai
yuun pir-e-mughan shekh-e-haram se hue yak-jan

mai-khane men kam-zarfi-e-parhez bahut hai
ik gardan-e-makhluq jo har haal men kham hai

ik bazu-e-qatil hai ki khun-rez bahut hai
kyuun mishal-e-dil 'faiz' chhupao tah-e-daman

bujh jaegi yuun bhi ki hava tez bahut hai
