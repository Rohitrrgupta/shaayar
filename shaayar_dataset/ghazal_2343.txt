hai shakl teri gulab jaisi
nazar hai teri sharab jaisi

hava sahar ki hai in dinon men
badalte mausam ke khvab jaisi

sada hai ik duriyon men ojhal
miri sada ke javab jaisi

vo din tha dozakh ki aag jaisa
vo raat gahre azaab jaisi

ye shahr lagta hai dasht jaisa
chamak hai us ki sarab jaisi

'munir' teri ghazal ajab hai
kisi safar ki kitab jaisi
