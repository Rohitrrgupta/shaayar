aise chup hain ki ye manzil bhi kaḌi ho jaise
tera milna bhi judai ki ghaḌi ho jaise

apne hi saae se har gaam laraz jaata huun
raste men koi divar khaḌi ho jaise

kitne nadan hain tire bhulne vaale ki tujhe
yaad karne ke liye umr paḌi ho jaise

tere mathe ki shikan pahle bhi dekhi thi magar
ye girah ab ke mire dil men paḌi ho jaise

manzilen duur bhi hain manzilen nazdik bhi hain
apne hi paanv men zanjir paḌi ho jaise

aaj dil khol ke roe hain to yuun khush hain 'faraz'
chand lamhon ki ye rahat bhi baḌi ho jaise
