dahr bhi 'mir' turfa-e-maqtal hai
jo hai so koi dam ko faisal hai

kasrat-e-gham se dil laga rukne
hazrat-e-dil men aaj dangal hai

roz kahte hain chalne ko khuban
lekin ab tak to roz-e-avval hai

chhoḌ mat naqd vaqt-e-nasiya par
aaj jo kuchh hai so kahan kal hai

band ho tujh se ye khula na kabhu
dil hai ya khana-e-muqaffal hai

sina-chaki bhi kaam rakhti hai
yahi kar jab talak mo'attal hai

ab ke hathon men shauq ke tere
Tak gareban men sar ko Daal ke dekh

dil bhi kya laq-o-daq jangal hai
hijr ba'is hai bad-gumani ka

ghairat-e-ishq hai to kab kal hai
mar gaya kohkan isi gham men

aankh ojhal pahaḌ ojhal hai
