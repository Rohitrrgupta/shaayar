kuchh yuun basar hui ki khuda yaad aa gaya

firdaus-e-gum-shuda ka pata yaad aa gaya

ik bevafa ka ahd-e-vafa yaad aa gaya

lekin jo vo ba-vaqt-e-dua yaad aa gaya
