hava ka zor hi kaafi bahana hota hai
agar charagh kisi ko jalana hota hai

zabani daave bahut log karte rahte hain
junun ke kaam ko kar ke dikhana hota hai

hamare shahr men ye kaun ajnabi aaya
ki roz khvab safar pe ravana hota hai

ki tu bhi yaad nahin aata ye to hona tha
gae dinon ko sabhi ko bhulana hota hai

isi umiid pe ham aaj tak bhaTakte hain
har ek shakhs ka koi Thikana hota hai

hamen ik aur bhari bazm yaad aati hai
kisi ki bazm men jab muskurana hota hai
