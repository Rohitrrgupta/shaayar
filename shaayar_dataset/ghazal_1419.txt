har qadam kahta hai tu aaya hai jaane ke liye
manzil-e-hasti nahin hai dil lagane ke liye

kya mujhe khush aae ye hairat-sara-e-be-sabat
hosh uḌne ke liye hai jaan jaane ke liye

dil ne dekha hai bisat-e-quvvat-e-idrak ko
kya baḌhe is bazm men ankhen uThane ke liye

khuub ummiden bandhin lekin huiin hirman nasib
badliyan uTThin magar bijli girane ke liye

saans ki tarkib par miTTi ko pyaar aa hi gaya
khud hui qaid us ko siine se lagane ke liye

jab kaha main ne bhula do ghair ko hans kar kaha
yaad phir mujh ko dilana bhuul jaane ke liye

dida-bazi vo kahan ankhen raha karti hain band
jaan hi baaqi nahin ab dil lagane ke liye

mujh ko khush aai hai masti shaikh ji ko farbahi
main huun piine ke liye aur vo hain khane ke liye

allah allah ke siva akhir raha kuchh bhi na yaad
jo kiya tha yaad sab tha bhuul jaane ke liye

sur kahan ke saaz kaisa kaisi bazm-e-samain
josh-e-dil kaafi hai 'akbar' taan uḌane ke liye
