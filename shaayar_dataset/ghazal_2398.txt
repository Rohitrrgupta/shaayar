jafaen dekh liyan bevafaiyan dekhin
bhala hua ki tiri sab buraiyan dekhin

tiri gali se sada ai kashanda-e-alam
hazaron aati hui charpaiyan dekhin

gaya nazar se jo vo garm tifl-e-atish-baz
ham apne chehre pe uḌti havaiyan dekhin

tire visal ke ham shauq men hon avara
aziiz dost sabhon ki judaiyan dekhin

hamesha mail-e-aina hi tujhe paaya
jo dekhin ham ne yahi khud-numaiyan dekhin

shahan ki kohl-e-javahar thi khak-e-pa jin ki
unhin ki ankhon men phirte salaiyan dekhin

bani na apni to us jang-ju se hargiz 'mir'
laḌain jab se ham ankhen laḌaiyan dekhin
