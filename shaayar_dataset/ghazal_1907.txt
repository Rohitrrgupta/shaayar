ab tujhe ai sitam-e-yar kahan se laun
pursish-e-hal pe hai khatir-e-janan maail

hai vahan shan-e-taghaful ko jafa se bhi gurez
nuur anqa hai shab-e-hijr ki tariki men

jalva-e-subh ke asar kahan se laun
sohbat-e-ahl-e-safa khuub hai maana lekin

sher mere bhi hain pur-dard va-lekin 'hasrat'
'mir' ka sheva-e-guftar kahan se laun
