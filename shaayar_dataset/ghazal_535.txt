vo khush-libas bhi khush-dil bhi khush-ada bhi hai
magar vo ek hai kyuun us se ye gila bhi hai

hamesha mandir-o-masjid men vo nahin rahta
suna hai bachchon men chhup kar vo khelta bhi hai

na jaane ek men us jaise aur kitne hain
vo jitna paas hai utna hi vo juda bhi hai

vahi amiir jo rozi-rasan hai aalam ka
faqir ban ke kabhi bhiik mangta bhi hai

akela hota to kuchh aur faisla hota
miri shikast men shamil miri dua bhi hai
