man bai-ragi tan anuragi qadam qadam dushvari hai
jivan jiina sahl na jaano bahut baḌi fankari hai

auron jaise ho kar bhi ham ba-izzat hain basti men
kuchh logon ka sidha-pan hai kuchh apni ayyari hai

jab jab mausam jhuma ham ne kapḌe phaḌe shor kiya
har mausam shaista rahna kori duniya-dari hai

aib nahin hai is men koi lal-pari na phul-kali
ye mat puchho vo achchha hai ya achchhi nadari hai

jo chehra dekha vo toḌa nagar nagar viran kiye
pahle auron se na-khush the ab khud se be-zari hai
