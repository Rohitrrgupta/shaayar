yaad use intihai karte hain
so ham us ki burai karte hain

pasand aata hai dil se yusuf ko
vo jo yusuf ke bhaai karte hain

hai badan khvab-e-vasl ka danggal
us ko aur ghair ko khabar hi nahin

ham lagai bujhai karte hain
ham ajab hain ki us ki banhon men

halat-e-vasl men bhi ham donon
lamha lamha judai karte hain

aap jo meri jaan hain main dil huun
mujh se kaise judai karte hain

ba-vafa ek dusre se miyan
jo hain sarhad ke paar se aae

vo bahut khud-satai karte hain
pal qayamat ke sud-khvar hain 'jaun'

ye abad ki kamai karte hain
