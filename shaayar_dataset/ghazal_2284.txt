zamin chhoḌ kar main kidhar jaunga
andheron ke andar utar jaunga

miri pattiyan saari sukhi huiin
nae mausamon men bikhar jaunga

agar aa gaya aaina samne
to apne hi chehre se Dar jaunga

vo ik aankh jo meri apni bhi hai
na aai nazar to kidhar jaunga

vo ik shakhs avaz dega agar
main khali saḌak par Thahar jaunga

palaT kar na paaya kisi ko agar
to apni hi aahaT se Dar jaunga

tiri zaat men saans li hai sada
tujhe chhoḌ kar main kidhar jaunga
