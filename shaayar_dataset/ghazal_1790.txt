dil men tire vo zauq-e-mohabbat nahin raha
phir naghma-ha-e-qum to faza men hain gunjte

tu hi harif-e-zauq-e-samaat nahin raha
aaiin kahan se aankh men atish-chikaniyan

main ab haris-e-gulshan-e-jannat nahin raha
shayad junun hai mail-e-farzangi mira

main vo nahin vo alam-e-vahshat nahin raha
mamnun huun main tera bahut marg-e-na-gahan

main ab asir-e-gardish-e-qismat nahin raha
jalva-gah-e-khayal men vo aa gae hain aaj

lo main rahin-e-zahmat-e-khalvat nahin raha
kya faeda hai dava-e-ishq-e-husain se

sar men agar vo shauq-e-shahadat nahin raha
