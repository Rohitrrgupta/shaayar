meri nava-e-shauq se shor harim-e-zat men
huur o farishta hain asiir mere takhaiyyulat men

meri nigah se khalal teri tajalliyat men
garche hai meri justuju dair o haram ki naqsha-band

meri fughan se rustakhez kaaba o somnat men
gaah miri nigah-e-tez chiir gai dil-e-vajud

gaah ulajh ke rah gai mere tavahhumat men
tu ne ye kya ghazab kiya mujh ko bhi faash kar diya

main hi to ek raaz tha sina-e-kaenat men
