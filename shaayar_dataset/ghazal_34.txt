hai ghazal 'mir' ye 'shifai' ki
ham ne bhi taba-azmai ki

us ke ifa-e-ahd tak na jiye
umr ne ham se bevafai ki

vasl ke din ki aarzu hi rahi
shab na akhir hui judai ki

isi taqrib us gali men rahe
dil men us shokh ke na ki tasir

aah ne aah na-rasai ki
kasa-e-chashm le ke juun nargis

ham ne didar ki gadai ki
zor o zar kuchh na tha to bar-e-'mir'

kis bharose par ashnai ki
