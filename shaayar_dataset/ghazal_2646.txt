kanch ke pichhe chand bhi tha aur kanch ke uupar kaai bhi
tinon the ham vo bhi the aur main bhi tha tanhai bhi

yadon ki bauchharon se jab palken bhigne lagti hain
sondhi sondhi lagti hai tab maazi ki rusvai bhi

do do shaklen dikhti hain is bahke se aine men
mere saath chala aaya hai aap ka ik saudai bhi

kitni jaldi maili karta hai poshaken roz falak
subh hi raat utari thi aur shaam ko shab pahnai bhi

khamoshi ka hasil bhi ik lambi si khamoshi thi
un ki baat suni bhi ham ne apni baat sunai bhi

kal sahil par leTe leTe kitni saari baten kiin
aap ka hunkara na aaya chand ne baat karai bhi
