tarash kar mire baazu uḌaan chhoḌ gaya
hava ke paas barahna kaman chhoḌ gaya

rafaqaton ka miri us ko dhyan kitna tha
zamin le li magar asman chhoḌ gaya

ajiib shakhs tha barish ka rang dekh ke bhi
khule dariche pe ik pul-dan chhoḌ gaya

jo badalon se bhi mujh ko chhupae rakhta tha
baḌhi hai dhuup to be-saeban chhoḌ gaya

nikal gaya kahin an-dekhe paniyon ki taraf
zamin ke naam khula badban chhoḌ gaya

uqaab ko thi gharaz fakhta pakaḌne se
jo gir gai to yunhi nim-jan chhoḌ gaya

na jaane kaun sa aaseb dil men basta hai
ki jo bhi Thahra vo akhir makan chhoḌ gaya

aqab men gahra samundar hai samne jangal
kis intiha pe mira mehrban chhoḌ gaya
