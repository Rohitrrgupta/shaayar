imtihan ke liye jafa kab tak
ghair hai bevafa pa tum to kaho

hai irada nibah ka kab tak
jurm malum hai zulekha ka

mujh pe ashiq nahin hai kuchh zalim
sabr akhir kare vafa kab tak

dekhiye khaak men milati hai
kahin ankhen dikha chuko mujh ko

na milaenge vo na aenge
hosh men aa tu mujh men jaan nahin

tu mujhe azmaega kab tak
tum ko khu ho gai burai ki

darguzar kijiye bhala kab tak
mar chale ab to us sanam se milen
