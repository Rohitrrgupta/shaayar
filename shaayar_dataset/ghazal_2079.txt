jab talak saans hai bhuuk hai pyaas hai ye hi itihas hai

khuun se tar-ba-tar kar ke har rahguzar thak chuke janvar
lakḌiyon ki tarah phir se chulhe men jal jo hua so hua

jo mara kyuun mara jo luTa kyuun luTa jo jala kyuun jala
muddaton se hain gham in savalon ke hal jo hua so hua

mandiron men bhajan masjidon men azaan aadmi hai kahan
