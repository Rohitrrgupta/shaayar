na jan-bazon ka majma tha na mushtaqon ka mela tha
khuda jaane kahan marta tha main jab tu akela tha

gharaunda yuun khaḌa to kar liya hai arzuon ka
tamasha hai jo vo kah den ki main ik khel khela tha

bahut saste chhuTe ai maut bazar-e-mohabbat men
ye sauda vo hai jis men kya kahen kya kya jhamela tha

agar taqdir men hota to ik din paar bhi lagta
ye dariya jhelne ko yuun to ai dil khuub jhela tha

hamesha hasrat-e-didar pe dil ne qanaat ki
baḌe dar ka mujavir tha baḌe murshid ka chela tha

kahan dil aur fusun-e-ishq ki ghaten kahan ya rab
na paḌta tha balaon men abhi kam-bakht anila tha

jahan chahe lage jis dil ko chahe chuur kar Daale
zaban se phenk maara baat thi naseh ki Dhela tha

tamasha-gah-e-duniya men bataun kya ummidon ki
tan-e-tanha tha main ai 'shad' aur relon pe rela tha
