daur-e-aghaz-e-jafa dil ka sahara nikla
hausla kuchh na hamara na tumhara nikla

tera naam aate hi sakte ka tha 'alam mujh par
jaane kis tarah ye mazkur dobara nikla

hosh jaata hai jigar jaata hai dil jaata hai
parde hi parde men kya tera ishara nikla

hai tire kashf-o-karamat ki duniya qaail
tujh se ai dil na magar kaam hamara nikla

lakhon men bas vahi allah ka pyara nikla
ibrat-angez hai kya us ki javan-margi bhi

haae vo dil jo hamara na tumhara nikla
'ishq ki lau se farishton ke bhi par jalte hain

sar-ba-sar be-sar-o-saman jise samjhe the vo dil
rone vaale hue chup hijr ki duniya badli

sham' be-nur hui subh ka taara nikla
aaj jis samt se vo dard ka maara nikla
