bahar-e-bagh ho miina ho jam-e-sahba ho
hava ho abr ho saaqi ho aur dariya ho

rava hai kah tu bhala ai sipahr-e-na-insaf
riya-e-zohd chhupe raz-e-ishq rusva ho

bhara hai is qadar ai abr dil hamara bhi
ki ek lahr men ru-e-zamin dariya ho

jo mehrban hain vo 'sauda' ko mughtanim janen
sipahi-zadon se milta hai dekhiye kya ho
