Dhab hain tere se baagh men gul ke
bu gai kuchh dimagh men gul ke

ja-e-roghan diya kare hai ishq
khun-e-bulbul charagh men gul ke

dil tasalli nahin saba varna
jalve sab hainge daagh men gul ke

is hadiqe ke aish par mat ja
mai nahin hai ayagh men gul ke

sair kar 'mir' is chaman ki shitab
hai khizan bhi suragh men gul ke
