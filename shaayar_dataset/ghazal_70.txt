shauq har rang raqib-e-sar-o-saman nikla
qais tasvir ke parde men bhi uryan nikla

zakhm ne daad na di tangi-e-dil ki ya rab
tiir bhi sina-e-bismil se par-afshan nikla

jo tiri bazm se nikla so pareshan nikla
kaam yaron ka ba-qadr-e-lab-o-dandan nikla

sakht mushkil hai ki ye kaam bhi asan nikla
dil men phir girye ne ik shor uThaya 'ghalib'

aah jo qatra na nikla tha so tufan nikla
kar-khane se junun ke bhi main uryan nikla

meri qismat ka na ek-adh gareban nikla
kuchh khaTakta tha mire siine men lekin akhir

jis ko dil kahte the so tiir ka paikan nikla
kis qadar khaak hua hai dil-e-majnun ya rab

laakh parde men chhupa par vahi uryan nikla
shokhi-e-rang-e-hina khun-e-vafa se kab tak

akhir ai ahd-shikan tu bhi pasheman nikla
jo na dekha tha so aine men pinhan nikla

main bhi mazur-e-junun huun 'asad' ai khana-kharab
peshva lene mujhe ghar se bayaban nikla
