sham-e-dil sham-e-tamanna na jala maan bhi ja
tez andhi hai mukhalif hai hava maan bhi ja

aisi duniya men junun aise zamane men vafa
is tarah khud ko tamasha na bana maan bhi ja

kab talak saath tira denge ye dhundle saae
dekh nadan na ban hosh men aa maan bhi ja

zindagi men abhi khushiyan bhi hain raanai bhi
zindagi se abhi daman na chhuḌa maan bhi ja

shahr phir shahr hai yaan ji to bahal jaata hai
shahr ko chhoḌ ke sahra ko na ja maan bhi ja
