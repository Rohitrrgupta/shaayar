tu samajhta hai ki rishton ki duhai denge
ham to vo hain tire chehre se dikhai denge

ham ko mahsus kiya jaae hai khushbu ki tarah
ham koi shor nahin hain jo sunai denge

faisla likkha hua rakkha hai pahle se khilaf
aap kya khaak adalat men safai denge

pichhli saf men hi sahi hai to isi mahfil men
aap dekhenge to ham kyuun na dikhai denge
