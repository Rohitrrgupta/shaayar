laḌ ke phir aae Dar gae shayad
bigḌe the kuchh sanvar gae shayad

sab pareshan dili men shab guzri
baal us ke bikhar gae shayad

kuchh khabar hoti to na hoti khabar
hain makan o sara o ja khali

yaar sab kuuch kar gae shayad
dil ko le kar mukar gae shayad

lohu ankhon men ab nahin aata
zakhm ab dil ke bhar gae shayad

ab kahin jangalon men milte nahin
be-kali bhi qafas men hai dushvar

kaam se baal o par gae shayad
shor bazar se nahin uThta

raat ko 'mir' ghar gae shayad
