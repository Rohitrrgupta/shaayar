kuchh din se intizar-e-saval-e-digar men hai
vo muzmahil haya jo kisi ki nazar men hai

sikhi yahin mire dil-e-kafir ne bandagi
rabb-e-karim hai tu tiri rahguzar men hai

maazi men jo maza miri sham-o-sahar men tha
ab vo faqat tasavvur-e-sham-o-sahar men hai

kya jaane kis ko kis se hai ab daad ki talab
vo gham jo mere dil men hai teri nazar men hai
