jo bhala hai use bura mat kar
khud se bhi barha mila mat kar

ye hai basti udaas logon ki
qahqaha maar kar hansa mat kar

baagh hai dil fareb donon se
phuul ko khaar se juda mat kar

roz ki laan-taan Thiik nahin
ghar men aine ko rakha mat kar

chehra mohara badalta rahta hai
itni jaldi bhi faisla mat kar
