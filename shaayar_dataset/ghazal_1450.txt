dil ko apne yuun hiin sanvarenge

ham bhi duniya pe laat marenge

koi duniya men dauḌe ya renge

haan vo chahenge to ubharenge
dil na dunga main aap ko hargiz

muft men aap jaan marenge

gul ko kya baghban sanvarenge
